Stages

Outfit

The Chuck E. bot originally wore the Cool Chuck outfit (red hat with black 'C' on it, blue shirt with yellow 'C' on it, and khaki shorts) when they opened in 2003 until it was replaced with the Avenger outfit in December 2010-Spring 2011. In September 2013 the Avenger Cap was removed to fit with the new Rockstar look and then in May 2015 the Avenger outfit was replaced by the Rockstar outfit which he wore until the store received the }}">2.0 Remodel.

Curtains/Random Movement

The curtains were disabled in late 2005, 2 years after they opened. In early 2013, Random Movements were turned on for the first time.
