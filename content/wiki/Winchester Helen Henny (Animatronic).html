+++ title = "Winchester Helen Henny (Animatronic)" draft = false tags = ["Animatronics"] date = "2024-05-23" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["WalterB","PizzaEntertainmentFanX","CECMentor"] +++ the Winchester Helen Henny Animatronic was an animatronic version of [Helen Henny]({{< ref "wiki/Helen Henny.html" >}}) created in August 1977 by [[Fantasy Forest|Fantasy Forest]] for the [[Pizza Time Theatre|Pizza Time Theatre]] on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}). The Bot was removed in February 1978, when [[Winchester Madam Oink (Animatronic)|Winchester Madam Oink]] was installed.

History

= Production

= In August 1977, Pizza Time Theatre had finalized their 'Guest Star' concept with the creation of Helen Henny. They tasked Fantasy Forest with creating the Bot.

= At Winchester

= The Bot was installed at the Pizza Time Theatre on [[San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)|370 S Winchester Blvd.]] on August 12th, 1977https://archive.org/details/atariincbusiness0000gold/page/256/mode/2up. The Bot was installed with the [Helen Henny Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Helen Henny Showtape.html" >}}). The Bot was replaced in February 1978 when [[Winchester Madam Oink (Animatronic)|Winchester Madam Oink]] was installed.

= Fate

= The fate of Winchester Helen is unknown as no photos of The Bot at [[Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office|Sunnyvale]], [[Pizza Time Theatre Cupertino, CA Corporate Office|Cupertino]], or any other PTT Corporate location have been found. The Bot is possibly the same as [[Kooser Helen Henny (Animatronic)|Kooser Helen Henny]], as the bots share the same design. The bot was most likely destroyed during the move to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.html" >}})

Design

The Bot had long brown hair, brown eyes, yellow hands with red painted fingernails, a black necklace with a gold medallion, and wears a white shirt with a purple vest over it.
