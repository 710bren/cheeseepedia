The building would later become a Pistol Pete’s Pizza that featured a Rock-Afire Explosion. This location is not to be confused with [Phoenix, AZ (8039 North 35th Ave)]({{< ref "wiki/Phoenix, AZ (8039 North 35th Ave).html" >}}), that opened on October 28th, 1983 with the Rock-afire explosion and is still open today.

Stages

This location first opened as a Chuck E. Cheese’s Pizza Time Theatre with a Balcony Stage. After the Pizza Time Theatre closed, the building was turned into a Pistol Pete’s Pizza with a [[Classic Stage|Classic Rock-Afire Explosion]]. {| class='wikitable' |+ !Stage !Installed !Removed !Notes |- |[[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] |1983 |1985 | |- |[[Classic Stage|Rock-Afire Explosion]] |1985-7 |2000 | |}
