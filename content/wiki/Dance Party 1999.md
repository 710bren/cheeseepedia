<b>Dance Party (April 1999)</b> released April 1999 and ran until July 1999.

Featured four segments featuring eight new songs and a remake from 1992! This was the last Studio C show to run on Laserdisc. 

<h2> Unused Blue Screen Footage </h2>
All the Studio C segments would be filmed by Brian Hagan, with the Chuck E. animatronic in front of a blue screen. This was done in case blue screen footage was ever needed, but ultimately went unused.<ref></ref> These were filmed on DVCAM tapes. 

On December 23, 2021, all the unused footage would be uploaded to Brian's YouTube channel.<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VTmxzgjgpmE</ref>

<h2> Digital Audio Masters </h2>
The showtape's original stereo audio for all the segments and the live show would be preserved onto Digital Audio Tape. On March 4, 2022, Brian Hagan would upload the original DAT recordings to his YouTube channel<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dSz9_hQ3ZJ4</ref>, while providing lossless FLACs of the audio to the community.

<h2> Trivia:</h2>

*Dancin' On The Moon is originally from [CEC Rock It In Space]({{< ref "wiki/CEC Rock It In Space.md" >}}),
*This was the final Studio C show to be produced on Laser disc and was also the final show to use the walkarounds to feature the rest of MMBB, Starting with Fabulous World Adventure they would use puppets.

*For Studio C 'Hip Mouse' and 'Dancin' On The Moon' were sang by Chuck E. Cheese. While on existing Munch sang 'Hip Mouse' and Jasper sang 'Dancin' On The Moon'
*This was the final show released for Studio C on laser disc.
