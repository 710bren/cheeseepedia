**_1270-1272 El Camino Real, Store #340**'Chuck E. Cheese 1 Stage that opened on May 30,
1994 with Chuck E. Cheese which is still open to this day.

This location received Concept Unification in 1994-2019, and received the 2.0 remodel and
Dance Floor in May 2019, As of August 2023 they still have the dance floor.

This location currently still has the 2.0 remodel.


## Stages ##
Originally when the store opened, they had a 1 Stage, Explosion as all other
locations did. They would have the RAE for a bit until 1994-2019, when they would undergo
Concept Unification, which involved turning the RAE into the Chuck E. Cheese characters.
In May 2019, San Bruno still has the 2019 model Dance Floor.
