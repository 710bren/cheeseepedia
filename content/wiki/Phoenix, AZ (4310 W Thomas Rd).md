**Phoenix, AZ (4130 W Thomas Rd)**  was a pizza time theater that opened somewhere in 1983 and closed in 1985. Not to be confused with [[Phoenix, AZ (8039 North 35th Ave)]], that opened on October 28th, 1983 with the Rock-afire explosion and is still open today.

##  Animatronics ## 
This location opened up with Madame Oink and had the Tune Machjne showtape. Like every other location, she eventually got replaced with broadway helen until its closure. They also had the king for a short time.
