The Beach Bear Mijjin was an animatronic produced by Creative Engineering Inc. for the New Rock-Afire Explosion in 1993. [1]

History

This character was produced in 1993 for the New Rock-Afire Explosion show.

Design

This bot is a standard Mijjin mech, the mech has blue eye pieces. The bot is the Beach Bear Character from the }}">Rock-Afire Explosion in the smaller Mijjin style, wearing Plaid Shorts, and a T-Shirt. The Shorts and T-Shirt design varies between Beach Bear bots.

Movements

All movements are on the Bottom Drawer. Eyes and eyelids are controlled by servos.
