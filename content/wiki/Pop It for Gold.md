## Gameplay ##
Pop It for Gold is a common game found at Chuck E. Cheese Locations, it is a game which tasks the player of popping a balloon inside the machine. This game is a test of Timing and Speed, Inside the machine is a wheel spinner that allows decides the air that goes into the Balloon on the side of the cabinet, 

There is a small section of the spinner that is named 'Instant Pop'. Upon the player hitting this, the balloon on the side of the machine will pop instantly. 

This is a considered the jackpot and a varying amount of tickets will be awarded to the player.
