Policy Manual is a lost manual for CEI employees. The only evidence of the manual's existence is a scan of the front cover art, drawn by Dave Thomas.1 The manual was half-letter sized, being 8.5' x 5.5'.2

Lost Media

The currently only known copy was located in Snapper's Blast to the Past Museum, which burned from a fog machine accident in October of 2010. The manual was not recovered from the rubble.34
