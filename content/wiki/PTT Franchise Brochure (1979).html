+++ title = "PTT Franchise Brochure (1979)" draft = false tags = ["Documents"] date = "2024-04-12" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" pageThumbnailFile = "THE GREAT ALL AMERICAN PIZZA SHOW BROCHURE.png" contributors = ["PizzaEntertainmentFanX"] +++ The 1979 PTT Franchise Brochure (Also called the 'THE GREAT ALL-AMERICAN PIZZA SHOW'https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf Brochure) was a promotional brochure created by [[Pizza Time Theatre|Pizza Time Theatre]] in Early 1979 to attract possible Franchisees. The Brochure contains info about assets, animatronics & items Franchisees would get for their PTT store. The Page had several images with captions reading about the information of each assets that Franchisee would be given.

= Back Cover

History

The Brochure was created in early 1979 after the opening of [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).html" >}}). It was made to attract possible Franchisees. The Brochure's stopped being used in probably late 1979 or early 1980.
