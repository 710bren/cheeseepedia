##Trivia:##
* The countdowns are reused from [September 2002 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2002 Show.md" >}}), 'Skateboarding' is reused from [January 2005 Show]({{< ref "wiki/January 2005 Show.md" >}}), 'Drawing Chuck E.' is reused from [August 1999]({{< ref "wiki/August 1999.md" >}}), VeggieTales - 'His Cheeseburger' is reused from [September 2001 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2001 Show.md" >}}) and 'Will Frye The Nutrition Guy' is reused from [Studio C Premiere]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Premiere.md" >}}).
* This show script was written by Matt Daniel.
* Munch was originally going to sing 'Baby I Need Your Loving' which is still alluded to in the dialogue both leading into and coming out of the song.
