The **Studio C Alpha Beta Mix** is a test stage found at [Chuck E. Cheese's]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese's.md" >}}) locations that opened from 2000 to 2001. it was the second Studio C stage to be created, after the Studio C Alpha. There were only 6 installed, 5 in the United States and one in [Jeddah, Saudi Arabia]({{< ref "wiki/Jeddah (Corniche Road), Saudi Arabia.md" >}}), with only 1 remaining as of September 2022 in Midland, Texas.
