**_2402 S Stemmons Fwy**_ is a _Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza_ that opened on October 27, 1992 with a [[2-Stage]] and is still open today with the 2.0 remodel. This store is known as the smallest corporate store. 

##  Stages ## 
Lewisville opened with a [\2](\1) with curtains, but this specific stage is unofficially known as a _Beta/Cappa Mix_. Around February 2022, the stage was removed for a 2021 Model [\2](\1) and [[Video Wall]].
