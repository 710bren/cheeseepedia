History

Melrose Park opened in September 2012 as the relocation of the old store on North Avenue. It featured a {{< wiki-link "Circles of Light" >}} and the 2010 update to the 2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative. The store remained mainly untouched since opening, besides the removal of the {{< wiki-link "Skytubes" >}}. As of August 2023, Melrose Park remains like this.
