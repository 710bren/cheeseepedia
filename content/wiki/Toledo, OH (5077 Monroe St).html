Cheese's location in Toledo, Ohio that opened on September 6th, 1993.

History

Toledo on Monroe opened on September 6th, 1993 along with the }}">5455 Airport Hwy location with the 1-Stage. The store would then received the 2005 Remodel in around 2006-2007 which included the removal of the Record Posters, Showroom Walls, & Dim Lighting. In around 2010-2011, the store expanded their gameroom, adding even more games. The store would then remain this way until they remodeled to 2.0 in around Late August 2021.
