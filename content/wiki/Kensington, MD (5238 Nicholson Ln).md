**Kensington, MD (5238 Nicholson Ln)** is an upcoming Chuck E. Cheese that is planned to open sometime in 2023. It will be a relocation from the [Rockville, MD]({{< ref "wiki/Rockville, MD (5270 Randolph Rd).md" >}}) location. The total size of the store will be 16,331 square feet.
