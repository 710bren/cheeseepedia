History

Bellevue opened sometime in or around February 1996 (day unknown). with a }}">1-Stage and the }}">Phase I remodel. Unfortunately, there isn't much info on Bellevue from around this time. In 2008/2009, Bellevue received the 2005 update to the 2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative, which involved the removal of 90s decor, showroom walls, balcony seating, and the addition of purple walls and an exterior change. As of March 2024, Bellevue remains like this.
