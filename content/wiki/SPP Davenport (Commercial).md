[\2](\1) This commercial was created in early 1981 and advertises the opening of the [\2](\1) ShowBiz Pizza Place. The commercial is 30 seconds long. It opens with [\2](\1) of the [\2](\1) location. The commercial then shows [\2](\1)’s Rock-afire Explosion show. The ad then shows people eating pizza, with a few seconds of the Billy Bob animatronic shown, then shows Fats Gorilla from the Wolf Pack 5, a woman enjoying some pizza with [\2](\1) visible in the background, and two people playing Whac-A-Mole. The ad then concludes with a ShowBiz Pizza Place logo on a blue background with lights around the logo flashing, and text above and below the logo saying “903 E. KIMBERLY DAVENPORT OPENS WEDNESDAY”.
