Trivia:

* This show debuted new character puppets for Helen and Munch; these puppets matched the Rockstar character designs more so than previous puppets. * Longtime Helen Henny voice actor Annagrey Labasse was replaced by Caroline Richardson in this show. * Birthday 2016 was the first birthday show to have the animatronics programmed during the countdown.
