History

Alexandria opened on November 20, 2010 with the [[CEC 2005 Remodel Program|2005 Remodel]] and a [Studio C Beta]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.html" >}}). Not much info is known about Alexandria in general due to lack of documentation. In July 2023, the store got 2.0, removing the Beta for a Dance Floor and Video Wall. As of August 2023, Alexandria is still open with 2.0.
