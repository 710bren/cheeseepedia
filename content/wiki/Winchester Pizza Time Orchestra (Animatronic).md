The **_Winchester Pizza Time Orchestra**_ was an animatronic produced by [[Fantasy Forest]] in 1977 as an animatronic version of the Pizza Time Orchestra at the first PTT on [[San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]].

##  History ## 
The Winchester Pizza Time Orchestra were produced in 1977 for the [[San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]] Store<ref>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf</ref>. Little is known about their usage in the store. They were either removed when Winchester got [[Cyberamics]] or when they closed in 1985.
