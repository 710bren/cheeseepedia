650 Bald Hill Rd, Store #645 is a Chuck E. Cheese's located in the Midland Commons Shopping Center in Warwick, Rhode Island, as a freestanding building. It opened on March 6, 2000, with a }}">Studio C Alpha.

This is not to be confused with the }}">Warwick, RI (1960 Post Rd) location, which opened in June 1984 and closed in Late 1990. It then relocated to }}">Warwick, RI (1245 Bald Hill Rd), opening in Late 1990, and closed in November 19, 1994.

History

The location opened March 6, 2000 in a freestanding building just outside the Rhode Island Mall (Now Midland Commons Shopping Center) with the }}">SPT 1992 Remodel Program as the first in CEC Rhode Island since the closure of }}">Warwick, RI (1245 Bald Hill Rd). This would be the first location in New England to receive any kind of Studio C stage.

On October 1, 2012 Warwick begin the }}">CEC 2005 Remodel Program. During the remodel, all aspects of previous remodels would be removed. Being replaced with the standard by 2012.[1]

Between April-May 2017, the Skytubes would be removed, Between July-August, Tokens would be replaced with }}">Chuck E.'s Play Pass.

On April 27, 2024, the Trampoline Zone was installed.[2]

On July 8, 2024, the store would begin its }}">2.0 Remodel, removing the stage for a }}">Video Wall and }}">Dance Floor.
