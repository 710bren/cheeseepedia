History

The location opened March 6, 2000 in a freestanding building just outside the Rhode Island Mall (Now Midland Commons Shopping Center) with the }}">SPT 1992 Remodel Program as the first in CEC Rhode Island since the closure of }}">Warwick, RI (1245 Bald Hill Rd). This would be the first location in New England to receive any kind of Studio C stage. On October 1, 2012 Warwick begin the }}">CEC 2005 Remodel Program. During the remodel, all aspects of previous remodels would be removed. Being replaced with the standard by 2012.(1) Between April-May 2017, the Skytubes would be removed, Between July-August, Tokens would be replaced with }}">Chuck E.'s Play Pass. On April 27, 2024, the Trampoline Zone was installed.(2) On July 8, 2024, the store would begin its }}">2.0 Remodel, removing the stage for a }}">Video Wall and }}">Dance Floor.
