**1555 New Circle Rd** was a Showbiz Pizza Place that opened on December 1, 1983 with a Rock-Afire Explosion, which is still open to this day.

This location received Concept Unification either in 1991 or 1992, which was replaced with a Studio C Beta, 16m in 2007 or 2008, and then received 2.0 in 2019.

##  History ## 
Lexington opened on December 1st, 1983 as the second Showbiz Pizza Place in Kentucky, after [\2](\1) which opened the year prior, in the Woodhill Circle Plaza. As of September 2022, they are the second last former Showbiz in Kentucky, as the Owensboro location closed in 1995. They were a franchise store, and were acquired by corporate on September 16, 1996, along with many other locations.

##  Stages ## 
Originally, Lexington had a Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz Pizza Place's did, they would have it for a few years, until either 1991 or 1992, when they would undergo a process known as Concept Unification, this was to replace the RAE with the Chuck E. Cheese characters. They would have the [\2](\1) until either 2007 or 2008, when they would replace it with a [\2](\1). They were the second 2.0 in the state, after [\2](\1), which received 2.0 in 2018. Lexington would be the most recent 2.0 in Kentucky until March of 2022, when [\2](\1) would receive 2.0, and then [\2](\1) received it in June of 2022, making Louisville the last non-2.0 store in Kentucky.
