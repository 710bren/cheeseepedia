**6065 Youngerman Circle** was a Showbiz Pizza Place that opened on December 22, 1981 with a Rock-Afire Explosion, which is still open to this day.

This converted to a Chuck E. Cheese around 1991, and converted to a Studio C Alpha and then received 2.0 in December 2021. It is the second and last Showbiz Pizza location to open in Jacksonville (7th in Florida). It is also known for having 2 surviving Showbiz Pizzas within the same city.
##History##
Jacksonville (Youngerman) opened December 22nd, 1981 as ShowBiz Pizza Place. It is located next to 3 hotels and it was also a franchised location. They would later end up receiving the [https://chuckepedia.com/wiki/1988_Remodel 1988 Remodel] removing old Showbiz decor, and being bought by corporate in August 31st, 1987 along with the , and . .<ref>https://youtu.be/5dWwX4Tk65g</ref> In 1991, Jacksonville (Youngerman) received Concept Unification which removed the Rock-Afire for the 3-Stage. Jacksonville (Youngerman) underwent another remodel in the year 2000 which involved the removal of the Neon Signage, Blue and White Awnings, some checkers around the store, and the removal of the 3-Stage for the Studio C Alpha. In 2006-2007, Jacksonville (Youngerman) received the [https://chuckepedia.com/wiki/CEC_2005_Remodel 2005 Remodel] which removing all 1990's store art, Balcony Seating, Showroom Walls, and adding purple walls. In 2020, Jacksonville (Youngerman) received the 2.0 remodel which removed the Alpha for the Dance Floor. As of January 2023, Jacksonville (Youngerman) still has the 2.0 Remodel and Dance Floor.
##Stages##
Originally when the store opened, they had a Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations had. In around 1991, Jacksonville (Youngerman) would undergo Concept Unification, turning the RAE into Munch's Make Believe Band. In 2000<ref>https://youtu.be/wCGEx3_pGgo</ref>, the 3-Stage was removed for the Alpha. In 2020, Jacksonville (Youngerman) received 2.0 and the Alpha was removed for the Dance Floor.
