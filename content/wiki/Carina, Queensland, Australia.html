+++ title = "Carina, Queensland, Australia" draft = false tags = ["Store List"] date = "2023-07-10" [Article] startDate = "1982-00-00" endDate = "1984-00-00" pageThumbnailFile = "Queensptt.png" contributors = ["CECBowlingGreen","CEC Tinley","CorrectorMan","BurgersNuggs445"] +++ 1151 Creek Rd was a [[Charlie Cheese's Pizza Playhouse|Charlie Cheese's Pizza Playhouse]] that opened in 1982 with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] and closed between 1984 and 1986. This store was a relocation of the [[Surfer's Paradise, Queensland, Australia|Surfer's Paradise]] location and would be the last location to open in Australia.

Stage

Charlie Cheese's would have a standard [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] and would have Cyberamic characters likely to have came from the [[Surfer's Paradise, Queensland, Australia|previous location]]. Because of this it is well to say that this location kept the Madam Oink character until they received the Bandstand Show in-around 1984. It is also unknown if they've also relocated the Dolli Dimples Cyberamic to this new location.

History

Little is known about this store as opposed to it's [[Surfer's Paradise, Queensland, Australia|original location in Surfers Paradise]] other than the location closing due to Pizza Time Theatre's bankruptcy.
