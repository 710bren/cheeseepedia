**_Uncle Klunk Showtape (Jeff Howell)**_ is an unreleased Rock-afire Explosion showtape originally intended to be released in ShowBiz Pizza Place locations in July 1983. It was replaced by the final [Uncle Klunk Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Uncle Klunk Showtape.md" >}}).
