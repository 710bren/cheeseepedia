**_3701 General Degaulle Dr**_ was a Showbiz Pizza Place that opened on February 23, 1981 with a Rock-Afire Explosion, which is still open to this day.

This converted to a Chuck E. Cheese around 1991, and still has the stage today. It is located in the Cypress Plaza Shopping Center. It is the 10th Showbiz Pizza to open and the 1st in Louisiana.
## History## 
New Orleans opened on February 23rd, 1981 as the 1st ShowBiz Pizza store in Louisiana with a Rock-afire Explosion show.  It is located next to 3 hotels and it was also a franchised location. They would later end up receiving the [https://chuckepedia.com/wiki/1988_Remodel 1988 Remodel] removing the old Showbiz decor, and adding newer decor. In 1991, New Orleans received Concept Unification which removed the Rock-Afire for the 3-Stage, removing all Showbiz Pizza decor for the new decor, and a change to the exterior. New Orleans underwent another remodel in the Late 90's removing older decor, another exterior change, and added SkyTubes. In 2006-2007, New Orleans received the [https://chuckepedia.com/wiki/CEC_2005_Remodel 2005 Remodel] which removed all 1990's store art, Balcony Seating, Showroom Walls, an exterior remodel, relocating the 3-Stage, and adding purple walls. As of January 2023, New Orleans still has the 2005 Remodel and the 3-Stage but has been listed for receiving 2.0.
## Stages## 
Originally when the store opened, they had a Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations had. In around 1991, New Orleans would undergo Concept Unification, turning the RAE into Munch's Make Believe Band. They still have this stage to this day.
