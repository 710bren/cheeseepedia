Cheese Legacy Summer Show 2024 Part 2 was an another summer show released on July 8, 2024 and was exclusively played in Northridge until July 28, 2024. It features a variety of songs released from 2017-2023. The show features “Together We’ve Got It” & “I Love Sparkles” as the only programmed songs in the playlist. This playlist also plays at Hicksville after their Grand Reopening on July 19th.
