Trivia:

*'Remember That Time' and 'Together We've Got It' are both from [[Chuck E. Cheese's Show 1 2017|Show 1 2017]]. *'Chuck E.'s Place 2017' is a modern remake of the [[CEC Jan '91|January 1991]] version of the song. *'Chuck E.'s Place 2017' was filmed at the Plano, TX Chuck E. Cheese. * All of the '40 Years of Fun' skits are only programmed for 3stage and Cyberamic stores. *An updated version of Show 3 2017 was released May 21st 2017 called [[Chuck E. Cheese's Show 3 2017 Part 2|Show 3 2017 Part 2]] which removed the Guiness Book Of World Records Part Blowers Promo replacing it with the 1990s 1 Minute Munch countdown and added 'Helen's Birthday' between 'How Far I'll Go' and 'New Cakes Promo'
