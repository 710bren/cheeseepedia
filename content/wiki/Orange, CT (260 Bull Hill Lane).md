<b><i>260 Bull Hill Ln</b></i> is a <i>Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza Time Theater</i> in Orange, Connecticut that opened in May 1983 and closed in 1985. It was located in an unnamed shopping plaza next to a former Kmart store that shares the same address. It was one of the last remaining Pizza Time Theaters in the state of Connecticut by the time it closed.

<h2>Stage</h2>
Orange opened with a [Balcony Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Balcony Stage.md" >}}) which remained until closure. The animatronics were sold at auction and their current whereabouts are unknown.
