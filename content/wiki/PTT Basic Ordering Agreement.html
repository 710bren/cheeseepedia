+++ title = "PTT Basic Ordering Agreement" draft = false tags = ["Documents"] date = "2024-05-20" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["The 64th Gamer"] +++ The Pizza Time Theatre Basic Ordering Agreement is a partially lost group of early [Pizza Time Theatre]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.html" >}}) documents detailing the early concepts of the company and its first two locations: [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}) and [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).html" >}}), likely dating between 1976-1977. The documents are currently in possession by Gene Landrum, and only a few photos of their contents have surfaced.

= Ordering

= Notably, all the pages seem to be put together by date. Page 3, while not denoting a specific date, does not give the cat character a name, which was later named sometime by the [[The Big Cheese Birthday Show Dialogue Recording Session (October 7, 1976)|The Big Cheese Birthday Show Dialogue Recording Session]] in October. The only outlier is Page 35, which details the costs of assembling animation hardware for the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}) location- though it being of financials may have it as part of a separate financial section of the documents.
