History

From the beginning of use of the [Animation Programming System]({{< ref "wiki/Animation Programming System.html" >}}) until August of 1992 the [APS Files]({{< ref "wiki/APS Files.html" >}}) would be stored for each stage variant of a showtape onto a single floppy disk. These disks would then be stored in cases and later labeled. These cases would stay in storage at the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.html" >}}) until the mid-2000's, where they would be thrown into the trash for unknown reasons. An employee, Matt Rivera, would notice this and decide to take the cases home for preservation. The cases would pass hands between 2 more fandom members before being archived online.

Showtapes

Cheese's Live 90]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese's Live 90.html" >}}) (December 31) * [Beach Bowzers '91 I]({{< ref "wiki/Beach Bowzers '91 I.html" >}}) (March 11) * [CEC Future]({{< ref "wiki/CEC Future.html" >}}) (March 14) * Concept Unification Diagnostics Tape 1 (March 14) * Concept Unification Diagnostics Tape 2 (March 14) * [CEC American Pride]({{< ref "wiki/CEC American Pride.html" >}}) (May 7) * CEC TV Classics '91 (June) * [CEC Games]({{< ref "wiki/CEC Games.html" >}}) (July 9) * [Land Of 1,000 Dances]({{< ref "wiki/Land Of 1,000 Dances.html" >}}) (September) * [CEC September 1991 Show]({{< ref "wiki/CEC September 1991 Show.html" >}}) (September 10) * [CEC Xmas 1991 Show]({{< ref "wiki/CEC Xmas 1991 Show.html" >}}) (December 6) * [CEC Rock It In Space]({{< ref "wiki/CEC Rock It In Space.html" >}}) (December 11) * Taking Care of Business, Rhyming Rap (January) * [Physical Fitness]({{< ref "wiki/Physical Fitness.html" >}}) (February 14) * [Environment '92]({{< ref "wiki/Environment '92.html" >}}) (April 4) * [Dance To The Music]({{< ref "wiki/Dance To The Music.html" >}}) (May) * [Children of America '92]({{< ref "wiki/Children of America '92.html" >}}) (June) * [Planes, Trains, And Automobiles]({{< ref "wiki/Planes, Trains, And Automobiles.html" >}}) (July 6) * Walking On Sunshine (August) * [Working For A Living]({{< ref "wiki/Working For A Living.html" >}}) (September 14) * [Birthday Star '92]({{< ref "wiki/Birthday Star '92.html" >}}) (September 22) Notably two shows are missing from the lineup that were released in this period, that being King '90 I (October 1990) and King '90 II (December 1990).
