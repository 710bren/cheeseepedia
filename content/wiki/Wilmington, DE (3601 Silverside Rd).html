+++ title = "Wilmington, DE (3601 Silverside Rd)" draft = false tags = ["Locations"] date = "2024-09-13" [Article] startDate = "1983-3-3" endDate = "" contributors = ["CECBowlingGreen","Rexburg090","CEC Tinley","47.17.236.200","CorrectorMan","Cheeseclassic","Caydenpedia"] [Location] latitudeLongitude = ["39.80897917","-75.54897198"] +++ 3601 Silverside Rd was a ShowBiz Pizza Place that opened on March 3, 1983, with a [[Classic Stage|Rock-Afire Explosion]], and closed as a Chuck E. Cheese's on October 12, 2020. This location received [Concept Unification]({{< ref "wiki/Concept Unification.html" >}}) in the early 1990's and replaced the 3-Stage with a [Circles of Light]({{< ref "wiki/Circles of Light.html" >}}) from [San Antonio, TX (6874 Ingram Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Antonio, TX (6874 Ingram Rd).html" >}}) in 2017. This location reopened as a Chuck E. Cheese on October 2, 2022, with a [[Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor)|Dance Floor]] + [Video Wall]({{< ref "wiki/Video Wall.html" >}}), which is still open to this day.

Stages

Originally when the store opened, they had a Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations had. In the early 1990's, the store would undergo Concept Unification, turning the RAE into Munch's Make-Believe Band. On September 16th, 1996, the store was bought out by Corporate. They would have the 3-Stage until September 2017, when they would receive the Circles of Light stage from San Antonio, TX (6874 Ingram Dr) because of a faulty sprinkler system; damaging the 3-Stage and causing its removal. They would have the COL stage until October 12, 2020, when they would close because of the COVID-19 pandemic.
