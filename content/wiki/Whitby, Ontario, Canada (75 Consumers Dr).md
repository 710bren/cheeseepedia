**_75 Consumers Dr**_ is an _Chuck E. Cheese's_ that opened on October 12, 2001 with a [Studio C Alpha]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Alpha.md" >}}), and is still open to this day.

## Stages ##
This location, as mentioned before, features a Studio C Alpha stage.  It is still at the location as of May 2024. 

## History ##
When it opened in 2001, the Whitby location was a Phase 3 store.  This changed in 2014.  Whitby currently has the 2012 update to the 2003 Cool Chuck Reconfiguration, which includes a new game package, the exterior being updated from the 1998 Cool Chuck exterior to a Rock Star Chuck exterior, and also has Rock Star Chuck on the artwork.
