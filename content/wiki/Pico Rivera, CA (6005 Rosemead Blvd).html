History

Pico Rivera opened sometime in 1982. Unfortunately, there is hardly any information about when it was a PTT, but they had a }}">Balcony Stage. Pico Rivera had the stage and the store unchanged until the late 80s when they received the 1988 remodel. They removed the Balcony Stage for the }}">Chuck E.'s House Facade and added new decor. They would remain like this until their second remodel in the mid-late 90's. They then removed the C-Stage for the }}">2-Stage removed the 1988 Remodel decor and added new decor and SkyTubes. They would remain like this until the installation of the }}">CEC 2005 Remodel between 2008-2009 when they removed the Showroom Walls, Balcony Seating, and relocated the 2-Stage. In 2010, the 2-Stage was converted into a 1-Stage. In March 2024, Pico Rivera began their }}">2.0 Remodel, removing their 1-Stage not long after for a 2021 Model Dance Floor. The store would also receive the Trampoline Zone Package. As of April 2024, Pico Rivera still has the }}">2021 Model Dance Floor and }}">Video Wall.
