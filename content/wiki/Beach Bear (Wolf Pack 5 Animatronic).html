Design

Beach Bear is a gray polar bear that plays an electric guitar. He wore a lei and swim trunks with varying colors depending on location.

Movements

* Mouth * Head left * Head right * Head up * Left eyelid raise * Right eyelid raise * Eye cross * Strum * Fret slide * Guitar raise * Foot tap
