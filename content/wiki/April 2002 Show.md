<b><i>April 2002 Show</b></i> was a show that released in April and ran through June 2002. 

This show featured 4 segments with 8 songs.

<h2> History </h2>
'Go To Chuck E. Cheese' and parts of 'Keep on Smiling' were filmed at the [Dallas (Montfort) TX]({{< ref "wiki/Dallas, TX (13364 Montfort Dr).md" >}}) location. 'Ease On Down the Road' was filmed at <i>SpeedZone Dallas</i>.

<h2>Trivia</h2>
* Eddie Coker's 'Woo Woo Dance' was reused from the [Just for Kids Show 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Just for Kids Show 2000.md" >}}) and 'Time to Fly' was reused from [Summertime 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Summertime 2000.md" >}}).
* In 'Go to Chuck E. Cheese' Chuck E. yells 'Come on Rob!'. This is a reference to Robert Gotcher.
* Chuck's curtains close during 'Everywhere' on all versions of the show.
