History

This location opened in December 2006 as a Chuck E. Cheese's with a Studio C Beta. It opened with the [CEC 2005 Remodel Program]({{< ref "wiki/CEC 2005 Remodel Program.html" >}}). They would shut down in May 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
