The show consisted of the main characters and moving props placed along the bannisters of the showroom, with the [[Winchester Warblettes (Animatronic)|Warblettes]] hung below the ceiling.

History

All characters were produced by [Fantasy Forest]({{< ref "wiki/Fantasy Forest.html" >}}), mainly by its owner Harold Goldbrandsen. Fantasy Forest was previously a mascot costume manufacturer, making the Winchester show their first animatronic products created. Atari's Cyan Engineering (also known as the Grass Valley Team) would handle the creation of the [Winchester Control System]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Control System.html" >}}), headed by Larry Emmons and Gary Waters.https://archive.org/details/atariincbusiness0000gold/page/328/mode/2up?q=theatre
