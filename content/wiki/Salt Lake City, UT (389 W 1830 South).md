**389 W 1830 South** is a Chuck E Cheese's opened on July 22nd 2013 with a [Circles of Light]({{< ref "wiki/Circles of Light.md" >}}){< ref "wiki/Circles of Light.md" >}}), and is still opened today, also it is the only Chuck E Cheese left in the state of Utah

This location is **not** a relocation of [Salt Lake City, UT (3344 South 1300)]({{< ref "wiki/Salt Lake City, UT (3344 South 1300).md" >}}) which was a pizza time theatre location opened in 1981, and closed sometime in the 1980s

## History ##
SLC opened with a [Circles of Light]({{< ref "wiki/Circles of Light.md" >}}) with a 16m bot which is still there to this day in October 2022 the bot would stop being supported.
