+++ title = "PTT Token 101 (gB,Bz, N)" draft = false tags = ["Pizza Time Theatre Tokens"] date = "2023-09-02" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["The 64th Gamer","CEC Tinley","2600:6C48:427F:F8C2:15F3:2D0B:8739:2C3E","ShowbizMidwest","2600:6C48:427F:F8C2:1807:59EB:EDEC:92E"] +++ The Pizza Time Theatre Token 101 was the first [Pizza Time Theatre]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.html" >}}) token to ever be released, which was first obtainable on May 3rd, 1977, at the [[Pizza Time Theatre Grand Opening Review (Event)|Pizza Time Theatre Grand Opening Review]] event for Atari employees.https://archive.org/details/the-gospel-of-st.-pong-revisited-vol-4-april-1977/page/n1/mode/2up They would be obtainable by the public on May 17th with the opening of first location in [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}). By 1978, they would be succeeded by the [[PTT Token 102 (Bz, N)|102 (Bz, N)]] and [[PTT Token 125 (gB, Bz, gBz, N)|125 (gB, Bz, gBz, N)]] tokens.
