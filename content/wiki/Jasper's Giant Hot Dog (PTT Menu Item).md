<b><i>Jasper's Giant Hot Dog</b></i> was a Pizza Time Theatre menu item introduced in 1979.<ref>https:<i>www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt</i>ptt_program_nc.pdf</ref><ref>https:<i>www.newspapers.com/article/the-napa-valley-register/49903763/</ref><ref>https:</i>www.newspapers.com/article/the-press-democrat/50027079/</ref><ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/fort-worth-star-telegram/55139481/</ref>

The item was a large hot-dog that came in a food basket with a cornichon pickle and chips. A smaller variant called the [Kiddie Dog]({{< ref "wiki/Kiddie Dog (PTT Menu Item).md" >}}) would be released later<ref name=':0'>https://texasarchive.org/2017_00873</ref>

<h2> History </h2>
It is unknown the price of the item upon its release. By 1982 it would be priced at $1.80<ref>https:<i>www.newspapers.com/article/the-cincinnati-post/98317675/</ref>, and by 1983 it would be priced at $1.99.<ref>https:</i>www.newspapers.com/newspage/135015382/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22&match=2</ref>
