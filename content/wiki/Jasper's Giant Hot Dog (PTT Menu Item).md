{{Menu Item|image=Gianthotdogmoment.png|menugroup=Sandwich|price=(1982) $1.80, (1983) $1.99|daterelease=1979|dateremove=1984}}**_Jasper's Giant Hot Dog**_ was a Pizza Time Theatre menu item introduced in 1979.<ref>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt//ptt_program_nc.pdf</ref><ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-napa-valley-register/49903763/</ref><ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-press-democrat/50027079/</ref><ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/fort-worth-star-telegram/55139481/</ref><ref>https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/135015382/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22&match=2</ref>

The item was a large hot-dog that came in a food basket with a cornichon pickle and chips. A smaller variant called the [\2](\1) would be released later<ref name=':0'>https://texasarchive.org/2017_00873</ref>

##  History ## 
It is unknown the price of the item upon its release. By 1982 it would be priced at $1.80, and by 1983 it would be priced at $1.99.
