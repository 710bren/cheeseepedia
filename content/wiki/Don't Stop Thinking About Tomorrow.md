**_Don't Stop Thinking About Tomorrow**_ was a show that released in April of 1995 at Chuck E. Cheese's locations. 

## Production ##

### Filming Locations ###

* All of the scenes with the interior of Chuck E. Cheese's was shot at the International Drive. Orlando, FL location.
* Everything that wasn't shot on location was filmed at Disney's, at the time, MGM Studios.
