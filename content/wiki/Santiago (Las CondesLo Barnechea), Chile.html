Av. San Josemaría Escrivá de Balaguer 12352, Lo Barnechea is a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened in Fall 1994 with a 1-Stage, which is still open to this day.

This location was the first location opened in the country of Chile, as well as being the oldest currently. They are also the only other 1-Stage outside of the United States, with }}">Plaza Oeste, which is also in Chile, being the other.
