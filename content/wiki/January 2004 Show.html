Trivia

*This show uses songs from previous shows as countdowns. 'Where is Chuck E.', 'The More We Get Together' and 'Home With A Range' are from [[Just for Kids Show 2000 | April 2000]] and 'Pizza Cabana' is from [Summertime 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Summertime 2000.html" >}}). *'Chuck E.'s Place' is a remake of a song from [CEC Jan '91]({{< ref "wiki/CEC Jan '91.html" >}}).
