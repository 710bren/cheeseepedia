Cheese's that opened on September 6th, 1993 with a [1-Stage]({{< ref "wiki/1-Stage.md" >}}). It still exists as of today, in good condition except for Helen's beak. This store is notable for being featured in a 2011 video where a Chuck E walkaround's head fell off during a Cupid Shuffle live show.
