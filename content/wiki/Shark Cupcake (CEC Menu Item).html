The Shark Cupcake was a limited time dessert item at Chuck E. Cheese locations as part of the Summer of Fun 2022 seasonal event.[1]

The item is a single vanilla cupcake, topped with grey icing and two white sugar wafers for eyes. A plastic shark fin inserted on the top and a custom cupcake liner designed to look like shark teeth covers the bottom.[3]
