**_Winchester Helen Henny**_ was a Guest animatronic featured at the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) Pizza Time Theatre. She would be replaced with [Winchester Madame Oink (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Madame Oink (Animatronic).md" >}}) during February 1978.

##Fate##
Her current whereabouts are unknown, but the most likely answer is she was destroyed in 1980 when Winchester installed their [Cyberamics]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics.md" >}}). Her design would later be reused for [Kooser Helen Henny (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Kooser Helen Henny (Animatronic).md" >}})

##Design##
Helen has long brown hair, brown eyes, yellow hands with red painted fingernails, a black necklace with a gold medallion, and wears a white shirt with a purple vest over it.

##Movements##

*Eyelids
*Mouth
*Eyes left and right.
*Head turn
*Head tilt up and down
*Hands
