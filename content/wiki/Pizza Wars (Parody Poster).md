**Pizza Wars** was a poster created for Showbiz Pizza and Chuck E. Cheese's in _1988_.

##History##
This poster was produced around the time of [Concept Unification]({{< ref "wiki/Concept Unification.md" >}}) and was placed for Showbiz Pizza Places and Chuck E. Cheese's. This poster was common in remodels at the time.<ref>https://cheeseepedia.org/wiki/1986_SPT_Remodel_Program</ref>

##Design##
The poster is a parody of a [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars_(film)|1977 Star Wars] movie poster from 1977.<ref>https://filmartgallery.com/products/star-wars-9859</ref>

It depicts Fatz, Mitzi Mozzarella, Dook LaRue, Billy Bob, and Beach Bear as the Star Wars actors.
