**_Reno, NV (651 E Moana Lane)**_ was a _Pizza Time Theatre_ that opened in December of 1982 with a Balcony Stage, which closed in 1983 before being reopened as a PTT-owned Zapp's Bar & Grill restaurant in 1983 and lasting to 1986-1987.

##History##

### Zapp's ###
On May 6th, 1986, Zapp's would host the _Fashion Auction Show_ in collaboration with _Show's West_. The event would take place from 9 to 11 pm, and feature a fashion auction, live entertainment, free prizes, makeovers, and a grand prize.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/image/149368772/?terms=%22zapp%27s%22&match=1</ref>
