## Summary ##
'New Games Falling Names' was a commercial that advertised Guitar Hero, Hat Trick, Jumpin' Fantasy, Big Bass Wheel, Red Hot, H2Overdrive, and Hammer 2 machines coming soon to locations.

## Production ##
This commercial was created by Zambrelli LLC and was mastered on March 29, 2010.
