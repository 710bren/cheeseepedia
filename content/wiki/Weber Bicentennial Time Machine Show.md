**The Bicentennial Time Machine Show** was a show made for Weber by **Creative Presentations Inc.** to advertise Weber Outdoor Grills. This show had three characters, those being **Kettle II** the Charcoal Powered Computer, **Weber Wabbit** the troublemaker, and **Professor Stephens** a stumblebum professor.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-daily-herald-cpi-history/141192335/</ref> The show would consist of the professor trying to get his computer to work while Weber Wabbit assumably kept causing trouble, and also spouted random facts about US Presidents throughout the show.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/southend-reporter-cpi-weber-show/141756471/</ref> At the end of each show, an 80 dollar weber grill (worth _$432.11_ as of 2024)<ref>https://www.bls.gov/data/inflation_calculator.htm</ref> would be given out to a random audience member.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/blue-island-sun-standard-cpi-weber-show/141756529/</ref> This was a travelling show, but it's main premiere seems to have been in the Ford City Shopping Center in Illinois<ref name=':0'>https://www.newspapers.com/article/blue-island-sun-standard-cpi-weber-show/141756529/</ref> with another showing being in Lafayette Square in Indiana.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-indianapolis-news-small-mention-of-t/141756634/</ref>
