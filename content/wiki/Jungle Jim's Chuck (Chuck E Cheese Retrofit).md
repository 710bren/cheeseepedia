**_Jungle Jim's Chuck**_ is a retrofit of [[PTT Chuck E. Cheese (Cyberamic)]] at _Jungle Jim's International Market_ in Cincinnati, Ohio. (5440 Dixie Hwy, Fairfield, OH 45014)

The bot was originally able to be seen in the 'Seafood' section of the grocery store located on top of the S.S Minnow on the drums, He was apart of the Nirobi Trio which was revealed to the public on September 11, 1995 apart of an 68,000 square foot expansion.

##  History ## 
Chuck originally came from the [[Cincinnati, OH (11400 Princeton Pike)]] Pizza Time Theatre and was bought by Jungle Jim's after the location closed in Fall 1984.<ref>[[Cincinnati, OH (11400 Princeton Pike)]]</ref>  Chuck was first installed in 1995 and was located atop of the 'S.S. Minnow' His stage was a boat. When he was first installed in 1995 the mask was identical to how Chuck appeared but without his teeth for the body was turned from a half bodied robot to full bodied drummer sitting on a stool. He wore a black vest and green pants with a yellow stripe going down the leg.
