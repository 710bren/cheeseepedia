Design

This CD Award is a parody of the popular 1969 Beatles album, Abbey Road. The art seen is a recreation of the album's cover with Chuck E. Cheese characters. Jasper, Helen, and Chuck E. are seen walking forwards on the crosswalk while Munch is seen walking in the other direction. In the Background are several vehicles parked on the side of the road. A large van on the left side is branded with 'Chuck E. Cheese Pizza'.
