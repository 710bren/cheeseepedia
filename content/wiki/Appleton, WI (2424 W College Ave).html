History

Appleton opened May 22, 1983 as ShowBiz Pizza Place in a unnamed shopping center. Unfortunately, information on Appleton is very scarce. In November 1985, the location closed down for unknown reasons, but it may be under performing, a reason for many other closures, After closure, it was split up into multiple businesses.

Stages

Originally when the store opened, they had a [[Classic Stage|Rock-afire Explosion]] , as all other Showbiz locations had, but it would only have it for 2 years as in November 1985, the location closed for unknown reasons. {| class='wikitable' |+ !Stage !Installed !Removed ! |- |Rock-Afire Explosion |May 22, 1983 |November 1985 |Last Showbiz Pizza Store to open in Wisconsin. |}
