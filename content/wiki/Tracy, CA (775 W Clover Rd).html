Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre that opened in 1983 (month and day unknown) with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] and closed in January 1995 (day unknown) with [Chuck E.'s House Facade]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E's House Facade.html" >}}). Not much info is really known about Tracy, but it was featured in footage from 1992 along with many other stores.

Stages

Tracy opened with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]], but we don't know much about it, along with [Chuck E.'s House Facade]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E's House Facade.html" >}}) when it was installed in 1989/1990. History

Tracy opened in 1983 (month and day unknown) with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] and the standard PTT remodel. However, information on Tracy as a PTT or in general is hard to find. In 1989/1990, they received the 1988 update to the 1986 SPT Remodel Program, which involved uniting the counters, merging the Fantasy Forest and lobby rooms, added checkers inside and outside of the store, replacing the decor, changed the location's name to Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza, and removed the Balcony Stage for [Chuck E.'s House Facade]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E's House Facade.html" >}}). In mid-1992, the store was featured in a reel along with [[Wilmington, NC (4389 Oleander Dr)|Wilmington, NC]], [[San Jose, CA (2445 Fontaine Rd)|San Jose (Fontaine/Tully), CA]], [[Albuquerque, NM (4418 Wyoming Blvd)|Albuquerque, NM]], [[Mesa, AZ (856 S Alma School Rd)|West Mesa, AZ]], [[Dothan, AL (3400 Ross Clark Circle)|Dothan, AL]], [[Harrisburg, PA (3883 Union Deposit Rd)|Harrisburg, PA]], [[Erie, PA (7200 Peach St)|Erie, PA]], [[Tallahassee, FL (2810 Sharer Rd)|Tallahassee, FL]], and [[Merced, CA (721 W Olive Ave)|Merced, CA]]. In 1995, the location closed permanently for unknown reasons. It was passed off to many businesses, and as of July 2023, the location ended up vacant again.
