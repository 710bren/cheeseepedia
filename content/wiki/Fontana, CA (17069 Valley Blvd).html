History

Fontana opened in November 1983 with a }}">Balcony Stage, which was to the right of the entrance. In 1988/89, they got the }}">SPT 1988 Remodel Program, retrofitting the Balcony Stage to }}">Chuck E.'s House Facade. Later in 1994/95, they received the SPT 1992/1993 Remodel Program, and got a }}">2-Stage. Then in late 2006, they received the }}">CEC 2005 Remodel Program, relocated the showroom, and got a }}">Studio C Beta. In April 2023, they received the }}">CEC 2018 2.0 Remodel Program removing the Studio C for a }}">Dance Floor + }}">Video Wall. As of October 2023, Fontana remains like this.
