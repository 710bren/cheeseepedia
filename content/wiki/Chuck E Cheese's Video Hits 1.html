Cheese's Video Hits #1 was a DVD given to CEC Corporate employees in September 2003 consisting of various showtape segments from the previous few years. The DVD itself is dated September 8, 2003. A second Video Hits DVD was never produced.

Video List

The main playlist features songs from September 2000 to September 2001. The songs are all in stereo, unlike their showtape counterparts.
