Trivia:

'Summertime Groove', 'Fun, Fun, Fun' and 'Good Old Summertime' are originally from [Summertime 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Summertime 2000.html" >}}), 'In My Pool' 'Goin' On a Picnic' and “Barbecue” are originally from [Summertime 2003]({{< ref "wiki/Summertime 2003.html" >}}), 'SPF' and 'Summertime' is originally from '[[Summer 2007 Show|Summer 2007.]] The only different between [Summer 2012]({{< ref "wiki/Summer 2012.html" >}}) and Summer 2012 (International) is the 'My Little Pony' intermission was replaced with Amphicar and 'Pound Puppies' was replaced with Closing Time?
