+++ title = "SPP Davenport (Commercial)" draft = false tags = [] date = "2022-09-22" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["ShowbizMidwest","Sponch man","Chouts1"] +++ This commercial was created in early 1981 and advertises the opening of the [[Davenport, IA (903 E Kimberly Rd)|Davenport, IA]] ShowBiz Pizza Place. The commercial is 30 seconds long.

Summary

The commercial opens with [[Fats Gorilla (Animatronic)|Fats Gorilla]] of the [Wolf Pack 5]({{< ref "wiki/Wolf Pack 5.html" >}}) at the [[Kansas City (Antioch), MO (2726 NE Vivion Rd)|Kansas City (Antioch), MO]] location. The commercial then shows [[Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd)|Jacksonville (Atlantic), FL]]’s Rock-afire Explosion show, followed by people eating pizza, with a few seconds of the Billy Bob animatronic shown. The commercial then shows Fats Gorilla from the Wolf Pack 5, a woman enjoying some pizza with [[Dingo Starr (Animatronic)|Dingo Starr]] visible in the background, and two people playing Whac-A-Mole. The commercial concludes with a ShowBiz Pizza Place logo on a blue background with lights around the logo flashing, and text above and below the logo saying “903 E.
