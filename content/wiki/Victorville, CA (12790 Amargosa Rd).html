History

Victorville opened on December 14, 1999, with the [[Phase 2 (1997)|Phase 2]] remodel and a [[Studio C (1997)|Studio C Alpha]]. A few years later between 2000 and 2002, they received [[Phase 3 (2000)|Phase 3]]. Around 2007, they received the 2005 update to the 2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative, which involved removing all 90s decor, removing showroom walls and balcony seating, expanded the store, added purple walls, and changed the exterior. The [Skytubes]({{< ref "wiki/Skytubes.html" >}}) were removed sometime in the mid-late 2010s. In April 2022, Victorville received the 2.0 remodel, removing the Studio C Alpha for a 2021 Model [[Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor)|Dance Floor]] and [Video Wall]({{< ref "wiki/Video Wall.html" >}}). The grand reopening occurred on June 2, 2022. As of August 2023, Victorville remains operational with the 2.0 remodel.
