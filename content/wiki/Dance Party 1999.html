Featured four segments featuring eight new songs and a remake from 1992! This was the last Studio C show to run on Laserdisc.

Unused Blue Screen Footage

All the Studio C segments would be filmed by Brian Hagan, with the Chuck E. animatronic in front of a blue screen. This was done in case blue screen footage was ever needed, but ultimately went unused.(1) These were filmed on DVCAM tapes. On December 23, 2021, all the unused footage would be uploaded to Brian's YouTube channel.(2)

Digital Audio Masters

The showtape's original stereo audio for all the segments and the live show would be preserved onto Digital Audio Tape. On March 4, 2022, Brian Hagan would upload the original DAT recordings to his YouTube channel(3), while providing lossless FLACs of the audio to the community.
