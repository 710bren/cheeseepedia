**_Show 1 2021**_ also known as the 'Evergreen show' is the generic show released on November 1, 2021, featuring a variety of songs released from 2016-2019

This was created with the idea to play between promotional events and would play from November 1, 2021 - November 14, 2021. This show features 8 Songs and added promo videos for the _Ryan FanFest_ event on November 27th, 2021.
