The Kickstarter would launch on October 28, 2020 by its programmer, Ryan Hayes, and would be cancelled on November 26, 2020.

Campaign

The campaign would start with the launch of the Kickstarter page, alongside a video on the Creative Engineering YouTube Channel promoting the project.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OuK-x3H_mJI The project asked for a $27,350, and planned the software's development to conclude by July 2021.
