Cheese's Pizza Time Theater that opened on March 15th, 1983 , with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] and closed in November 1996 (day unknown), as a Chuck E. Cheese's with a [2-Stage]({{< ref "wiki/2-Stage.html" >}}). This location should not be confused with the Rochester, NY (3130 Ridge Rd) location, which opened on July 5th, 2003 with a [Studio C Beta]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.html" >}}), and is still open to this day with the 2.0 remodel.
