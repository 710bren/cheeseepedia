**2250 Coastal Lane** is a [Chuck E. Cheese's]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese's.md" >}}) that opened on April 26, 2011 with the [Galaxy Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Galaxy Stage.md" >}}), which is still open to this day

The Galaxy Stage is a test stage that was only installed in this location, and was the predecessor to the [Circles of Light]({{< ref "wiki/Circles of Light.md" >}}) stage.
