**_965 S Opdyke Rd** was a ShowBiz Pizza Place located in Pontiac, Michigan._

## History ##
Pontiac opened its doors on November 4th, 1982 as the 4th ShowBiz Pizza in Michigan. They opened with a Rock-afire Explosion like every other ShowBiz did. It had a typical SPP layout seen in many other stores across the United States. After two years of operation, the store permanently closed on January 8, 1984.
