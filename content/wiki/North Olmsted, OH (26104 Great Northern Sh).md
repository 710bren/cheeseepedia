**North OImsted, OH (26104 Great Northern Sh)** is a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened on Jan 18 1993 with a . It should not be confused with [North Olmsted, OH (23333 Lorain Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/North Olmsted, OH (23333 Lorain Rd).md" >}}), which was the Pizza Time Theatre which was convered to Marc's Funtime. 

## History ##
When it opened, North Olmsted featured a . The original showroom was located in the right corner of the store, across from the kitchen. 

In 2004, the store would undergo a remodel, which would see the 2 Stage removed and the showroom moved across the store to the left gameroom, next to the prize counter. The new showroom would feature a .

In August 2017, North Olmsted would receieve the 2015 remodel, along with  as both stores were under the same DM who ordered the remodels. The store would keep it's original exterior, unlike Parma which got the updated logo and exterior.\



In December 2018, North Olmsted became the first store in Ohio to get the 2.0 remodel. The store was expanded into the available store space next door. The new expansion became a third game room. The showroom and  were moved back to its original spot in the far right of the store. The bathrooms were also relocated at this time.
