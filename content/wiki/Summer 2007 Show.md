## Trivia ##

* Birthday Extravaganza 07 (Birthday Star Extravaganza 2007) is featured exclusively on Studio C discs and was re-edited since the April 2007 Show. Currently unknown if it was playable.

* Matt Daniel wrote 'I Love The Sunshine' for this show.
* This is the last known CEC showtape to be released on the SVHS format. It is unknown if the [September 2007 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2007 Show.md" >}}) was released on SVHS, and the [Holiday 2007]({{< ref "wiki/Holiday 2007.md" >}}) show was released on DVD for Studio C and Existing Stages.
