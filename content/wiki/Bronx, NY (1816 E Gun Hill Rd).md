**1816 E Gun Hill Rd** is Chuck E. Cheese's that opened on February 2, 2003 with a [\2](\1) in front of The Home Depot. This location will be a 2.0 Sign of 2021.

##  History ## 

The Chuck E. Cheese's in Bronx, NY (1816 E Gun Hill Rd) was opened on February 2, 2003 will be a Phase 3 Sign with a Studio C Beta the store would receive the 2010 Update to the [[2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative]]; keeping the store's exterior as is, In around 2016, the store would remodel once again; yet again, the store's exterior would remain untouched. This was removed during the COVID-19 Pandemic the building remains vacant, with removed the avenger logo, will be getting a 2.0 Logo of the building in 2021.
