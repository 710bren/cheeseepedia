**_2755 E Grapevine Mills Circle, Store #854**_ is a _Chuck E. Cheese's_ that opened on May 21st, 2002 with a . They received the 2.0 remodel in November 2022. it is located in a freestanding building in the Grapevine Mills.

## History ##
Grapevine opened on May 21st, 2022 in a freestanding building located within the Grapevine Mills. This was one of 21 . In 2020, Chuck E.'s Funhouse would be removed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. In November 2022, Grapevine would remodel to the 2.0 Remodel, removing the Studio C for a 2021 Model Dance Floor + Video Wall but keeping the booths and most other elements from their 2015 Remodel. Grapevine's Grand Reopening would occur on December 15th, 2022 . As of May 2023, Grapevine still has the 2021 Model Dance Floor + Video Wall.
