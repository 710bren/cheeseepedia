Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre locations, which operated from March 1981 up until round 1984 to 1986.

History

In 1981, PTT opened there first Charlie Cheese's in [[Surfer's Paradise, Queensland, Australia|Surfer's Paradise, Queensland, Australia (2 Cavil Avenue)]] with a [Cyberamics Portrait / Shelf Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Portrait Shelf Stage.html" >}}). The name was changed from Chuck to Charles due to Chuck meaning 'Throw Up' in Australian. The Cavil Location closed in 1982 & another one opened the same year in [[Carina, Queensland, Australia|Carina, Queensland, Australia (1151 Creek Road)]] with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Balcony Stage.html" >}}).The location closed around 1984 to 1986 making Charlie Cheese's defunct.
