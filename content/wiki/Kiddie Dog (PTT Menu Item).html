The item was a small hot-dog that came in a food basket.https://www.newspapers.com/image/710969464/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20pizza%20time%20theatre&match=2

History

The hot-dog would start at $0.99 in price upon release.https:www.newspapers.com/image/135015382/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22&match=2 By October 20, 1983, it had gone up to $1.29.https:www.newspapers.com/image/274053727/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22%20%2299%22%20%22hot%20dogs%22%20%221%20free%20token%22%20&match=1 The item seemingly would be discontinued by 1984, though briefly makes an appearance at a New York location in 1988 with fries as $0.99, likely as a localized variant.https://www.newspapers.com/image/710969464/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22&match=2
