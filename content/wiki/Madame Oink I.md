|pttreel=y}}**_Madame Oink I**_ was a showtape featured at the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) _Pizza Time Theatre_ in February of 1978.

The tape came with the installation of [Winchester Madame Oink]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Madam Oink (Animatronic).md" >}}) at the location.

##Show List##
It is unknown what the exact order of the segments were on the original tape, as they were uncovered on D2-Tape outside of the standard showtape format.

The **_Madame Oink Birthday**_ was later found on the 'Pizza Time (Birthday 8-TR)' (November 13, 1978) ('Pizza Time (Birthday 8-TR)') 8-Track tape amongst other songs.
