History

The location originally started as a Country Boy Market store, before being remodeled at the cost of $600,000 in 1980 into a Pizza Time Theatre. During the planning of the restaurant in April of 1980, it was announced to be opening around June 15th of that year.[2] This would later be pushed back to July/August.[4] Tom Warnshius, a former manager of the Old Spaghetti Factory, would be the opening manager of the location.[5]

The location would later close in May of 1985.
