+++ title = "Fresno, CA (5384 N Blackstone Ave)" draft = false tags = ["Store List"] date = "2023-09-30" [Article] startDate = "1980-00-00" endDate = "1985-00-00" pageThumbnailFile = "Fresno King 1984.avif" contributors = ["The 64th Gamer","2600:4040:5A0E:D900:80EC:F561:38B:60E"] +++ Fresno, CA (5384 N Blackstone Ave) was a Pizza Time Theatre that opened around July/August of 1980 with a [Cyberamics Portrait / Shelf Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Portrait Shelf Stage.html" >}}), which closed in May of 1985.https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-fresno-bee-fresno-jun-25-1985/86368897/ This is not to be confused with the Showbiz Pizza store on [[Fresno, CA (3117 W Shaw Ave)|3117 W Shaw Ave]] that opened on October 28th, 1982 with a [[Classic Stage|Rock-afire Explosion]] and is still operational as a Chuck E. Cheese's with a Studio C Alpha as of September 2023 and the [[Fresno, CA (7809 N Blackstone Ave)|current N Blackstone Ave]] location that opened on September 24th, 2002 with a [[Studio C Beta|Studio C Beta, 32m]] and is also still operational as of September 2023.

Stages

The store would open with a [Cyberamics Portrait / Shelf Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Portrait Shelf Stage.html" >}}) that lasted throughout the store's run. It also had a lounge with [[The King / King Kat (Lounge)|The King]] and a Cabaret with [[Dolli Dimples (Cabaret)|Dolli Dimples]].https:www.newspapers.com/article/the-fresno-bee/70294307/https:www.newspapers.com/article/the-fresno-bee/70294427/

History

The location originally started as a Country Boy Market store, before being remodeled at the cost of $600,000 in 1980 into a Pizza Time Theatre. During the planning of the restaurant in April of 1980, it was announced to be opening around June 15th of that year.https:www.newspapers.com/article/the-fresno-bee/70294427/ This would later be pushed back to July/August.https:www.newspapers.com/article/the-fresno-bee-fresno-aug-21-1980/86368771/ Tom Warnshius, a former manager of the Old Spaghetti Factory, would be the opening manager of the location.https://www.newspapers.com/image/704387472/?clipping_id=70294464&fcfToken=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJmcmVlLXZpZXctaWQiOjcwNDM4NzQ3MiwiaWF0IjoxNjk2MDI4NTEwLCJleHAiOjE2OTYxMTQ5MTB9.gghD60ugArJw7eOEIbLGlx_tuBLKKC6maVdtbo-fkOg The location would later close in May of 1985.
