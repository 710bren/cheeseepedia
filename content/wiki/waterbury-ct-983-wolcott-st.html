It was at auction before everything was thrown out. This location is not to be confused with the {{< wiki-link "Waterbury, CT (650 Wolcott St)" >}} location, which opened on September 29, 1992, and closed on June 25, 2020. This location was the first in the New England region. It was located in the 2 Brothers Plaza. It had a {{< wiki-link "Cyberamics Balcony Stage" >}}, with only a few photos of it. As of March 2024, the location is a Goodwill Waterbury Store & Donation Station.

Stages

Waterbury opened with a {{< wiki-link "Cyberamics Balcony Stage" >}} which stayed until closure. The animatronics are unknown to what happened with them after closure.
