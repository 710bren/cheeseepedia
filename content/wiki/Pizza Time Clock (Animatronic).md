The **Pizza Time Clock** is a prop created by Garner Holt Productions, Inc. for Chuck E. Cheese's locations, manufactured from 1997-2012.

The Pizza Time Clock is used on the , [Studio C Beta]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.md" >}}){< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.md" >}}), and [CU Beta 16M]({{< ref "wiki/CU Beta 16M.md" >}}){< ref "wiki/CU Beta 16M.md" >}}). The design is different on the Beta stages.
