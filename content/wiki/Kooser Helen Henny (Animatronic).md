**Kooser Helen Henny** was a Guest Star animatronic featured at the [\2](\1)

##  Design ## 
Kooser Helen is an upgraded version of [[Winchester Helen Henny]]. She has long brown hair, brown eyes, and wears a black necklace with a gold and black medallion. She wears a white shirt with a purple vest. She carries a guitar and has yellow hands with red fingernails. 

##  Movements ## 

* Eyelids
* Mouth
* Eyes left and right
* Head turn
* Head tilt
* Hands

##  Fate ## 
[\2](\1)
After Kooser upgraded to the Balcony show, Helen's whereabouts were unknown. It's assumed she was moved to [\2](\1), but unlike [\2](\1), there are no pictures of her at Sunnyvale. She is currently presumed to have been destroyed when Sunnyvale relocated to [\2](\1) after PTT's bankruptcy.
