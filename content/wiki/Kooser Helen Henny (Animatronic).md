**Kooser Helen Henny** was a Guest Star animatronic featured at the 

## Design ##
Kooser Helen is an upgraded version of [Winchester Helen Henny]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Helen Henny.md" >}}). She has long brown hair, brown eyes, and wears a black necklace with a gold and black medallion. She wears a white shirt with a purple vest. She carries a guitar and has yellow hands with red fingernails. 

## Movements ##

* Eyelids
* Mouth
* Eyes left and right
* Head turn
* Head tilt
* Hands

## Fate ##

After Kooser upgraded to the Balcony show, Helen's whereabouts were unknown. It's assumed she was moved to , but unlike , there are no pictures of her at Sunnyvale. She is currently presumed to have been destroyed when Sunnyvale relocated to  after PTT's bankruptcy.
