<h2>Trivia</h2>
* All of the countdowns in this show are reused. Countdowns 1, 2, 5 and 6 are from [CEC TV April 2005 Show]({{< ref "wiki/CEC TV April 2005 Show.md" >}}). Countdowns 3 and 4 are from [January 2005 Show]({{< ref "wiki/January 2005 Show.md" >}}). Countdown 7 is from [Summer 2004 Show]({{< ref "wiki/Summer 2004 Show.md" >}}).
*The Coke Spot is reused from [September 2004]({{< ref "wiki/September 2004.md" >}}).
* Charlie Rockit - 'Sports' is reused from [September '94]({{< ref "wiki/September '94.md" >}}). It does not feature any of the background music and sound effects present in the original release.
* This is the first show with no new curtain programming for any stage.
* This is the last show to reuse segments and live shows with programming for the [CU Flower]({{< ref "wiki/CU Flower (Animatronic).md" >}}). After this show, this stage effect would go unused.
