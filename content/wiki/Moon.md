<b><i>Moon</b></i> is one of the prop characters in the [Rock-afire Explosion]({{< ref "wiki/Rock-afire Explosion.md" >}}), having made his debut as an animatronic at [Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd).md" >}}).

<h2> History </h2>

<h2>= Origins & Showbiz Pizza Place </h2>=
Moon is most commonly depicted with a masculine posh voice courtesy of Burt Wilson. Even though he rarely talks outside of backing vocals, he seemed to care deeply about the law. Moon was the only characters to remain unchanged during [Concept Unification]({{< ref "wiki/Concept Unification.md" >}}).

<h2>= Present Day </h2>=
Moon was occasionally used as an announcer on the [Concept Unification Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Concept Unification Stage.md" >}}). As of 2024, Moon is used for female backing vocals for Chuck E. Cheese songs. Original parts for Moon would start to be reproduced by Creative Engineering's new employees in 2023.
