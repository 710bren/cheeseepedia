+++ draft = false title = "Jungle Jim's International Market" startDate = "1971" endDate = "" pageThumbnailFile = "" contributors = ["Chouts1"] citations = [" "] alsoKnownAs = [] tags = ["History"] categories = ["Jungle Jim's"] +++ Jungle Jim's International Market (Also known as just Jungle Jim's) is a chain of grocery stores in the Cincinnati, Ohio area founded by "Jungle" Jim Bonaminio. The stores are known for being large in size, containing a large selection of domestic and foreign foods. They also contain various displays and attractions, including various animatronics both created for the store and purchased from other locations in Ohio.
