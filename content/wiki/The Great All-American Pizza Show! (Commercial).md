The **_Great All-American Pizza Show! 60s**_ was a 60 second commercial that aired in local television stations in 1979 to promote the 'The Great All American Pizza Show!' media promotion. 
##Cast##
*It is rumored that Crusty's voice actor portrayed Chuck E.'s voice in the commercial.
##Notes##
*The commercial was filmed at the _[San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}){< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}})_ and _[San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}}){< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}})_ locations.
*Whilst the only surviving audio from a radio advertisement only advertises the _[San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}})_ and _[San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}})_ locations, it was mentioned in the 79' brochure that the ad would also advertise potential franchisee stores.
*The ad features up-close shots of the __ and __ animatronics.
*The dialogue is different then the one featured in the _[The Great All-American Pizza Show! (Radio Ad)]({{< ref "wiki/The Great All-American Pizza Show! (Radio Ad).md" >}})_ , although shares many lines.
