## History ##
Dover opened on December 27, 2002 as the last of 3  remodel. Around 2005, the location received a minor remodel, removing the  stage. Between 2009 and 2011, the dim lighting was removed and the exterior was replaced with a standard [2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative]({{< ref "wiki/2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration  Major Remodel Initiative.md" >}}) exterior. The [Skytubes]({{< ref "wiki/Skytubes.md" >}}) were also removed somewhere presumably in 2019. As of July 2023, Dover remains like this.
