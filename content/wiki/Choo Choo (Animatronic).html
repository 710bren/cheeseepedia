History

Choo Choo featured in Rock-afire Explosion shows first debuting in 1980 at the }}">Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd) on August 14th of that year. Choo Choo would later be converted to Munch Jr. during }}">Concept Unification, now resembling a smaller Mr. Munch with a propeller cap. Munch Jr. would additionally gain a spotlight used from the original }}">Antioch spotlight. He would later lose his spotlight with the }}">September '94 showtape, as stores were instructed to switch the spotlight out with a gobo light that faced the center stage curtains. The last official Munch Jr. in a store was at the Wilmington, North Carolina location, which would close in October of 2020.
