The Signage Update for Pizza Time Theatre in 1981.


## History ##
In May 1981 It is announced that PTT would be updating all Trademarks. Along with announcing that San Francisco based, S&O Consultants<ref>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/documents/ptt/ptt_pizzatimes1-1.pdf</ref> would be redesigning their logos, exteriors, d√©cor, and signage. 

In the coming months this package would roll out to locations through the summer of 1981.

## Design ##
This, being the first major logo change in Pizza Time Theatre, exteriors were redesigned to have more of an impact on the guest. The has exterior some theatrical elements, such as Marquee lights, Colors, and Material Choice.

The Signs included in this package were rounded, rectangular signs. The borders are either white or red with and the inside being colored either Green, Blue, Red, or Black.
