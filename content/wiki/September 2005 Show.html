Trivia:

*Chuck's curtains close during 'Mayberry' on the existing stages. *The backgrounds for the Studio C puppets are their regular ones, but without the usual diagonal line. *The Studio C puppets return to only appearing during the dialogue portions between songs. *The Fundraising Spot is reused from [September 2004]({{< ref "wiki/September 2004.html" >}}) with that show's logo replaced with the September 2005 logo. *'Spring Cleaning' is reused from [Spring Break]({{< ref "wiki/Spring Break.html" >}}) and the kaleidoscope countdown is reused from [September 2003 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2003 Show.html" >}}) * The countdowns reuse footage from [April 2002 Show]({{< ref "wiki/April 2002 Show.html" >}}), [September 2002 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2002 Show.html" >}}) and Footloose *The Play-Doh spot uses the instrumental of 'What Up' from [September 2004]({{< ref "wiki/September 2004.html" >}})
