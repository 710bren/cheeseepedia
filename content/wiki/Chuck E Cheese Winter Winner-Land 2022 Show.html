Cheese Winter Winner-Land was the holiday show originally released on November 21, 2022 and was played through January 8, 2023. The show was creation in conjunction with the Winter Winner-land promotion. This show features seven Holiday songs, of which all were reused. The automatic live show is, 'Chuck E.'s Yuletide Slide'.
