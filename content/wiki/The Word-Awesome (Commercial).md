'The Word-Awesome' is a commercial created in 2002 that featured the interior of a decorated Chuck E. Cheese's. This is one of the only commercials for the 2000s to have walkaround shown.

## Summary ##
The commercial is mostly children playing games or on rides saying the word 'Awesome'; along with a few appearances from Chuck E. Cheese in animation and physical form.
