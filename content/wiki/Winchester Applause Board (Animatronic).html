+++ title = "Winchester Applause Board (Animatronic)" draft = false tags = ["Animatronics"] date = "2024-05-21" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["PizzaEntertainmentFanX","CECMentor"] +++ The Winchester Applause Board was an animatronic created by [Fantasy Forest]({{< ref "wiki/Fantasy Forest.html" >}}) in 1977 for the first Pizza Time Theatre on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}})https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf. the bot was programmed using the [Winchester Hardware Setup]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Hardware Setup.html" >}}) & Controlled using the [Winchester Control System]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Control System.html" >}}). The Bot was removed when Winchester closed in 1985 & sent to the [Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office.html" >}}) where it remained until the move to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.html" >}}).

History

The bot was produced in 1977 by Fantasy Forest. The bot was put in the Pizza Time Theatre on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}) somewhere in 1977. The bot was at Winchester until 1985, when it was removed & most likely sent to the [Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office.html" >}}). The bot was most likely thrown out when PTT was moving to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.html" >}}).

Design

Not much is known about the design of the bot. The bot had a Wood barrier around several clapping animal arms.
