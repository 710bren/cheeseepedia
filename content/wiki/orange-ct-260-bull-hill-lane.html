Cheese's Pizza Time Theater in Orange, Connecticut that opened in May 1983 and closed in 1985. It was located in an unnamed shopping plaza next to a former Kmart store that shares the same address. It was one of the last remaining Pizza Time Theaters in the state of Connecticut by the time it closed.

Stage

Orange opened with a {{< wiki-link "Cyberamics Balcony Stage" >}} which remained until closure. The animatronics were sold at auction and their current whereabouts are unknown.
