The ****The Great All-American Pizza Show !' Radio Ad<ref>Link To The Ad Audio [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X08DBu9jAwg]</ref>**_ was a standard radio advert / commercial created in early 1979 to promote the [Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.md" >}}) locations in [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}}) & [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) on Bay Area radio stations. There is also an alternate take of the ad with a different disembodied voice-over.<ref>Link To The Alt-Take [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jeFGpTbOlEc]</ref>

## History ##
The Ad was created in 1979 (Day & Month Unknown) along with [The Great All-American Pizza Show! (Commercial)]({{< ref "wiki/The Great All-American Pizza Show! (Commercial).md" >}}) to promote the PTT stores in [San Jo]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}})[se, California]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}}). The ad was most likely used until either latex 1979 or early 1980, though it could have ran longer. The ad was discontinued by, 1981, when the 'The Great All-American Pizza Show!' slogan was replaced with 'Smile America Say Chuck E. Cheese'.
