Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre that opened in December 1981 (day unknown) and closed in 1984 (month and day unknown) with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. This is not to be confused with [Bakersfield, CA (3760 Ming Ave)]({{< ref "wiki/Bakersfield, CA (3760 Ming Ave).html" >}}), which opened on November 16, 1982 as a Showbiz Pizza and is still open today with 2.0.

Stage

Bakersfield opened with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. Not much is known about this stage, and was later auctioned after closure.

History

Bakersfield opened in December 1981 (day unknown) with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. This predated the Bakersfield SPP by 11 months. Nothing is known about this location at all, however. The store would close in 1984 (month and day unknown) due to underperformance. As of today, the store is now part of a TJMaxx.
