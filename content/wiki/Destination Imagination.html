Production

= Filming Locations

= * All of the scenes with the interior of Chuck E. Cheese's were shot at the International Drive, Orlando, FL location. * Everything that wasn't shot on location was filmed at Disney's Hollywood Studios, then known as Disney-MGM Studios at the time.
