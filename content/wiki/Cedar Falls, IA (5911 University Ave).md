**_5911 University Ave Ste. 100**_ is a _Chuck E. Cheese's_ that opened on August 1, 2001 with a [[Studio C Beta]] and is still open today with the 2.0 remodel.

This was the relocation of the [\2](\1) and closed in January 2001 (day unknown) with a [\2](\1).

##  Stages ## 
Cedar Falls at first had a [[Studio C Beta]] with a 32m bot. The stage would get minor modifications until September 2022, when it was removed as part of the 2.0 remodel.
