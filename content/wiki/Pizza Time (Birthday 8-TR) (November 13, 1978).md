**_Pizza Time (Birthday 8-TR)**_ is an audio reel containing various audio segments from unknown Pizza Time Theatre showtapes, recorded November 13, 1978.

## Tracks ##

# [Madame Oink I]({{< ref "wiki/Madame Oink I.md" >}}) Birthday Skit
# Birthday skit featuring Mr. Munch, though from a currently unknown showtape.
# 'The Sidewalks of New York', most likely a clip from the [Kooser Helen Henny Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Kooser Helen Henny Showtape.md" >}}), though possibly also a clip from the unconfirmed [Kooser Helen Henny 2nd Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Kooser Helen Henny 2nd Showtape.md" >}}).
