|ceibetacam=y|image=Uncleklunkdiagtape.png}}The <b><i>Uncle Klunk Diagnostic Showtape</b></i> is a July 1983 diagnostic tape for [Uncle Klunk]({{< ref "wiki/Uncle Klunk (Animatronic).md" >}}) using the [CEI Betamax]({{< ref "wiki/CEI Betamax.md" >}}) format to display an identical diagnostic video alongside the [animation data]({{< ref "wiki/Data.md" >}}).

The only segment, <i>Pizza Expert</i>, was reused from the [Uncle Klunk Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Uncle Klunk Showtape.md" >}}) bundled in the same package Uncle Klunk was shipped with.
