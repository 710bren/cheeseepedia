Waterbury CT's Pizza Time theater opened in April 1982 and closed in 1984. Not to be confused with the 650 Wolcott St location witch opened on September 29, 1992 which closed on June 25, 2020 This location was the first in the New England region. It was located on 983 Wolcott St In the 2 brothers plaza. It had a Balcony stage with only a few photos of. 

As of today this location is a Goodwill thrift store, Its located next to a Pet Smart, Stop & Shop, Taco Bell, Game Stop, and a Crunch Fitness.
