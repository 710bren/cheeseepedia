State St, Store #3203 was a franchised Chuck E. Cheese's location that opened in Summer of 1996 with a 1-Stage and closed in 2014. This location is not a relocation of the Sandy, UT (792 E 9400) which was a Pizza Time Theatre location that opened in February 1983 and closed in April 1985.

Stage

This location received the Cyberamic characters from the [[Riverside, CA (4033 Chicago Ave)|Riverside, CA]] location for use on their 1-Stage show. The Cyberamics still utilized a portion of the original cosmetics before removal. Once the store closed in 2014, the stage was cleared of everything, and a large projector screen was put on the stage. The Cyberamics' parts would then be used at the [[Orem, UT (40 E Center St)|Orem]] store on their 3-Stage show. {| class='wikitable' |+ !Stage !Installed !Removed |- |1-Stage |Summer 1996 |2014 |}

History

As this franchised store opened in 1996 in a secluded location, it has gone mostly undocumented besides of things seen in the 2001 home video that would suggest the store opened with [[Phase 1 (1994)|Phase I]].https://youtu.be/9AQTyNYsy2w At some point the store also received [[Phase 3 (2000)|Phase III]], which most likely is why the [Skytubes]({{< ref "wiki/Skytubes.html" >}}) were relocated between then and 2011. In September 2011 this location was remodeled for the first time as a Chuck E. Cheese's during the [[2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative|'Cool Chuck' remodel initiative]].

= Closure

= By the Fall of 2014, the location closed and was turned into an expansion of the next-door All-Star Bowling franchise. All of the walls were repainted, and all CEC branding would be removed, although various games would be reused,https://youtu.be/5HSeQVUvLn4 most notably the [Skytubes]({{< ref "wiki/Skytubes.html" >}}). The Showroom, however, remained, along with the stage (without the bots or props) the artifact shelves, the booths, and the hanging lights.
