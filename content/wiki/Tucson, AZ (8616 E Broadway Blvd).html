This is not to be confused with the [Tucson, AZ (5534 E 22nd St)]({{< ref "wiki/Tucson, AZ (5534 E 22nd St).html" >}}) Pizza Time Theatre that opened on June 15th, 1980, and closed in 1987 with a [[Cyberamics Portrait / Shelf Stage|Portrait Stage]]. This is also not to be confused with the [Tucson, AZ (6130 E Speedway Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/Tucson, AZ (6130 E Speedway Blvd).html" >}}) location which opened in April 1995 with a 1-Stage and relocated to [Tucson, AZ (6125 E Speedway Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/Tucson, AZ (6125 E Speedway Blvd).html" >}}) in October 2014 with a Circles of Light, 16m and still operates to this day.
