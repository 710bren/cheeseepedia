**_Jeddah CornIche Road**_ was a _Chuck E. Cheese's_ that opened in December 12th, 2001 with a Studio C Alpha Beta Mix, which closed in July of 2019.
[[File:JeddahCornch2018Exterior.png|thumb|Jeddah's exterior in 2018.]]
This location was known as the world's largest Chuck E. Cheese location till closing in 2019.

## Stage ##
[[File:JEDDAHCORNSTAGEI2002SEPT.png|thumb|Jeddah's stage in 2002.]]
Jeddah had a unique Studio C stage that used the Blue screen and center monitor from an alpha on the left in the middle had the Alpha Beta Mix with The Pizza Phone and on the right of that had the Blue screen and monitor from the alpha reversed. This also featured two blue screen areas and two interactive consoles.
