The **_Pizza Time Theatre Happy Holidays Mug**_ was a Christmas themed mug released around the holiday season of 1983.
##Design##
The cup is a 3 inch tall glass mug with a metal plaque glued on the front. This plaque presents Chuck E. Cheese dressed as Santa Claus on a sleigh. The sleigh has two elves and a bag of presents on it. Below the art is the text:<blockquote>HAPPY HOLIDAYS
