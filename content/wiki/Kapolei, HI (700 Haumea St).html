History

Kapolei began construction around 2015/2016 and was a learning experience for the franchisee. The location finished in 2018 and later opened the same year. This ended up being an interesting location, containing the }}">sky tubes (a bigger Chuck E’s Clubhouse variant) and Avenger-era costumes. As of 2024, Kapolei remains like this.
