+++ title = "Winchester Warblettes (Animatronic)" draft = false tags = ["Animatronics"] date = "2024-03-23" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["WalterB","NoName2000","PizzaEntertainmentFanX"] +++ The Winchester Warblettes Animatronic was an animatronic produced in 1977 by [Fantasy Forest]({{< ref "wiki/Fantasy Forest.html" >}}) for the [Pizza Time Theatre]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.html" >}}) location on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}). The bot used the [Winchester Hardware Setup]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Hardware Setup.html" >}}) for programming & the [Winchester Control System]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Control System.html" >}}) for controlling the bots movements. The bot was replaced in 1981 when Winchester got [Cyberamics]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics.html" >}}).

History

= Production & Manufacturing

= Before the bot was created, Harold Goldbrandsen created concept art showing what the bot could do. The concept art had scrapped movements for the eyes, heads, & hands. The bot was developed in early 1977 by Harold Goldbrandsen & Fantasy Forest.

= Location Use

= The bot was installed in [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}) & Programmed to the [Pizza Time Theatre Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Showtape.html" >}}) in Early 1977. The bot was later programmed to the [Helen Henny Showtape]({{< ref "wiki/Helen Henny Showtape.html" >}}), the [[Madame Oink I|Madame Oink Showtape]], & the [[Foxy Colleen I|Foxy Colleen Showtape]] from August 1978 to November 1978. The bot was seen in an episode of the Australian broadcast series, 'Four Corners' in their 'New Eatery Fad' segmenthttps://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ucg8MujPDQA.

= Fate

= The bot was removed in 1981 when 'Winchester' got Cyberamics. The bot was sent to the [Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office.html" >}}) the same year & left there until 1984, when they were thrown out during the move to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.html" >}}).

Design

The Figures had 2 Eyes, black feathers, a yellow beak, black fluffed hair feathers, yellow feet, a yellow neck chain, & a brow purse.
