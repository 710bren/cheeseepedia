<h2>Trivia:</h2>
*Chuck's curtains close during 'Mayberry' on the existing stages.
*The backgrounds for the Studio C puppets are their regular ones, but without the usual diagonal line.
*The Studio C puppets return to only appearing during the dialogue portions between songs.  
*The Fundraising Spot is reused from [September 2004]({{< ref "wiki/September 2004.md" >}}) with that show's logo replaced with the September 2005 logo.
*'Spring Cleaning' is reused from [Spring Break]({{< ref "wiki/Spring Break.md" >}}) and the kaleidoscope countdown is reused from [September 2003 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2003 Show.md" >}})
* The countdowns reuse footage from [April 2002 Show]({{< ref "wiki/April 2002 Show.md" >}}), [September 2002 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2002 Show.md" >}}) and Footloose
*The Play-Doh spot uses the instrumental of 'What Up' from [September 2004]({{< ref "wiki/September 2004.md" >}})
