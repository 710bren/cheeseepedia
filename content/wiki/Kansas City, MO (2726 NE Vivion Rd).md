**_2726 NE Vivion Rd**_ was a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened as a ShowBiz Pizza Place on March 3, 1980 as the first ShowBiz Pizza Place location to open.

It closed in 2006.

##  History ## 
###  ShowBiz Pizza ### 
It opened on March 3, 1980, in the Antioch Center. This location used to have the [[Wolf Pack 5]], and later replace by the [[The Rock-afire Explosion]] animatronic band.
