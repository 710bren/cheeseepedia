**_Tree Tops from Helen Henny**_ was a segment programmed at CEC Corporate by _Matt Daniel_ for fun.

The segment's audio was the song 'Tree Tops' by Eisley<ref>https://archive.org/download/unlistedcec/Tree%20Tops%20from%20Helen%20Henny.mp4</ref>, and programmed by Matt on the 3-Stage using . The song was uploaded to Matt's YouTube channel in 2006, though it was later unlisted and then turned private.


## Creation ##
The segment was programmed sometime between 2002 and mid-2005, as that the time in which Matt was the programmer for shows. Not much else is known about the segment's creation.
