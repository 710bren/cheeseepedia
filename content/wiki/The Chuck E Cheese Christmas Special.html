Trivia:

*The intermission music in this show comes from an album called Contempory Christmas by Ron Roy. *Santa's voice actor, Ron Schneider, previously voiced Mr Mouth. *'Run Run Rudolph' is sung by Munch, but was programmed to Jasper. This song was also used as a live show, which also includes the error.
