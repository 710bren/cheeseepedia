The Rockin Rollies was an obscure animatronic pizza place that opened in Sioux Falls South Dakota on June 13th of 1990<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/image/239986137/?match=1&terms=The%20Rockin%20Rollies%20Animated%20Stage%20Show&clipping_id=142570522</ref> and closed at an unknown date. . The owners of the business were Galaxy Enterprise Inc<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/image/239990369/?match=1&clipping_id=148089196</ref> members Randy Koepsell, Randy Hindbjorgen, Chris Kirkus, and Doug McBeth.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/image/239986698/?match=1&clipping_id=148088987</ref> The business closed on July 31st of 1991<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/image/240035399/?match=1&clipping_id=148089387</ref> after failing to beat local competition, and also likely after receiving public backlash for using video lottery machines and serving alcohol at a children's pizza place. <ref>https://www.newspapers.com/image/239995538/?match=1</ref>
