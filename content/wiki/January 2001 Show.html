Trivia

*The Department 18 logo appears in 'Wacky Pirates in The Outfield.' *This show's logo reuses clips from various shows released in 1999-2000. This includes 'Hip Mouse' from [April 1999]({{< ref "wiki/April 1999.html" >}}), 'Do Your Ears Hang Low' 'Are You Eating Mr. Munch' 'The Muffin Man' and 'Song of Sixpence' from [Just for Kids Show 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Just for Kids Show 2000.html" >}}), 'Pizza Cabana' from [Summertime 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Summertime 2000.html" >}}), and 'Chuck E.' from [September 2000 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2000 Show.html" >}}). *The Studio C version of the show logo has the Studio C logo. This is edited out in the Existing Stage version though it can still be seen faintly in the background.
