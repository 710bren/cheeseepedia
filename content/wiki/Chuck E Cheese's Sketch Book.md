## Gameplay ##
Chuck E. Cheese's Sketch Book is a Common Game found inside Chuck E. Cheese Restaurants, They are typically located in the [Toddler Zone]({{< ref "wiki/Toddler Zone.md" >}}) and is very common to be found in the Toddler Zone, This game rewards no tickets but instead gives the user a printed copy of a photo that the machine takes, upon activation, The Machine will greet you and ask the user to choose a portrait or self photo by pressing one of the buttons in front of the user, upon selection of either of these options, the machine will start a countdown from 10, upon reaching 0. The Machine asks the user to 'Smile for Chuck E.'. Once this process is done, it will thank you for using it and informs you that your picture is being printed and will be prepared in less than a minute. Music plays for about 1 minute while your photo is printing, After around 10 - 15 seconds, The user's picture will be dispensed from a cubbyhole at the bottom of the machine.
