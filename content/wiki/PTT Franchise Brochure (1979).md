The **_1979 PTT Franchise Brochure**_ (Also called the **'THE GREAT ALL-AMERICAN PIZZA SHOW**'<ref name=':0'>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf</ref> **_Brochure**_) was a promotional brochure created by [_Pizza Time Theatre_]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.md" >}}) in Early 1979 to attract possible Franchisees.

The Brochure contains info about assets, animatronics & items Franchisees would get for their PTT store. The Page had several images with captions reading about the information of each assets that Franchisee would be given.

## Contents<ref name=':0' /> ##

### Front Cover ###
The Front Cover has the Standard 1978/1979 small size PTT Logo with text under it saying 'THE GREAT ALL-AMERICAN PIZZA SHOW!'. The Front Cover also has artwork of [Chuck E. Cheese]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese.md" >}}) with a Cigar & Microphone, [Pasqually]({{< ref "wiki/Pasqually.md" >}}), [Jasper T. Jowls]({{< ref "wiki/Jasper T Jowls.md" >}}) playing his Banjo, [Mr. Munch]({{< ref "wiki/Mr Munch.md" >}}) eating a pizza, & The Mopsey Sisters.

### Inside Pages ###
The Inside Pages contain several images with captions. Here's a list of them:

* [Kooser Chuck Walkaround]({{< ref "wiki/Kooser Chuck Walkaround.md" >}}) with text saying 'You got a genuine Chuck E. Cheese<sup>c</sup> costume for promotions, entertainment, & all special occasions.'
* [Winchester Chuck E. Cheese V2 (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Chuck E Cheese V2 (Animatronic).md" >}}), [Kooser Mr. Munch (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Kooser Mr Munch (Animatronic).md" >}}), [Winchester Jasper T. Jowls (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Jasper T Jowls (Animatronic).md" >}}), Kooser Mopsey Sisters (Animatronic), [Winchester Foxy Colleen (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Foxy Colleen (Animatronic).md" >}}), & [Winchester Pasqually (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Pasqually (Animatronic).md" >}}) with text saying 'The entire star-studded [Cyberamic]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics.md" >}})<sup>c</sup> cast of Pizza Time Theatre, The Pizza Time Players, are ready to appear year 'round  at your franchise.'
* The 1977-1981 Long sized PTT Logo
* Blueprints
* Brochures & a tape
* An Employee with standard PTT employee attire on
* 2 Stacks of 1978 PTT Tokens
* An Arcade Game
* [Winchester Flag Wavers (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Flag Wavers (Animatronic).md" >}}), & [Winchester Pizza Time Orchestra (Animatronic)]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Pizza Time Orchestra (Animatronic).md" >}})
* A PTT Franchise Book
* A Pizza with some toppings surrounding it
* and PTT merchandise

### Extra Page ###
An extra page shows the dining area of the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) location in 1979.

### Back Cover ###
The Back Cover had the PTT Inc. Logo from 1978 on a plain white background.

## History ##
The Brochure was created in early 1979 after the opening of [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}}). It was made to attract possible Franchisees. The Brochure's stopped being used in probably late 1979 or early 1980.
