The **_1979 PTT Franchise Brochure**_ (Also called the **'THE GREAT ALL-AMERICAN PIZZA SHOW**' **_Brochure**_<ref name=':0'>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf</ref>) is a promotional brochure created by [[Pizza Time Theatre]] in Early 1979 to attract possible Franchisees.

The Brochure contains info about assets & items Franchisees would get for their PTT store. The Page had several images with captions reading about the information of each assets that Franchisee would be given.

##  Contents<ref name=':0' /> ## 

###  Front Cover ### 
The Front Cover has the Standard 1978/1979 small size PTT Logo with text under it saying 'THE GREAT ALL-AMERICAN PIZZA SHOW!'. The Front Cover also has artwork of [[Chuck E. Cheese]] with a Cigar & Microphone, [[Pasqually]], [[Jasper T. Jowls]] playing his Banjo, [[Mr. Munch]] eating a pizza, & [[The Mopsey Sisters]].

###  Inside Pages ### 
The Inside Pages contain several images with captions. Here's a list of them:

* [[Kooser Chuck Walkaround]] with text saying 'You got a genuine Chuck E. Cheese<sup>c</sup> costume for promotions, entertainment, & all special occasions.'
* [[Winchester Chuck E. Cheese V2 (Animatronic)]], [[Kooser Mr. Munch (Animatronic)]], [[Winchester Jasper T. Jowls (Animatronic)]], [[Kooser Mopsey Sisters (Animatronic)]], [[Winchester Foxy Colleen (Animatronic)]], & [[Winchester Pasqually (Animatronic)]] with text saying 'The entire star-studded Cyberamic<sup>c</sup> cast of Pizza Time Theatre, The Pizza Time Players, are ready to appear year 'round  at your franchise.'
* The 1977-1981 Long sized PTT Logo
* Blueprints
* Brochures & a tape
* An Employee with standard PTT employee attire on
* 2 Stacks of 1978 PTT Tokens
* An Arcade Game
* [[Winchester Flag Wavers (Animatronic)]], & [[Winchester Pizza Time Orchestra (Animatronic)]]
* A PTT Franchise Book
* A Pizza with some toppings surrounding it
* and PTT merchandise

###  Extra Page ### 
An extra page shows the dining area of the [[San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]] location in 1979.

###  Back Cover ### 
The Back Cover had the PTT Inc. Logo from 1978 on a plain white background.

##  History ## 
The Brochure was created in early 1979 after the opening of [[San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)]]. It was made to attract possible Franchisees. The Brochure's stopped being used in probably late 1979 or early 1980.
