<b><i>Little Rock, AR (2706 S Shackleford Rd)</b></i> <b><i>Store #671</b></i> is a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened on April 28th, 2014 with a Circles Of Light.

This location is a 'Phase 5' store with Rockstar artwork.

<h2>History</h2>
This location opened on April 28th, 2014, as a relocation of the old Little Rock store located on [10901 N Rodney Parham Road 303]({{< ref "wiki/Little Rock, AR (10901 N Rodney Parham Road 303).md" >}}). It opened with a Circles Of Light stage with no animatronic, being the first in Arkansas to have no animatronics.

<h2>Stages</h2>
The store opened with a Circles Of Light with no animatronic, which is still there to this day.
