+++ title = "Opening Team Pin (90's)" draft = false tags = ["Employee Wear"] date = "2023-04-21" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" pageThumbnailFile = "Newhartfordnypin2.avif" contributors = ["The 64th Gamer"] +++ The Opening Team Pin (Avenger) was given to CEC employees for being the first employees during a store's opening, to be worn on their lanyard.

Design

The design is a metal pin featuring Coach Chuck art. The bottom section of the pin says 'Opening Team', alongside a section for the name of the store. The store name seems to be white plastic embedded into the metal with the name pressed and printed into it.

= Store Variations

= * 'New Hartford, NY' ([New Hartford, NY (4631 Commercial Dr)]({{< ref "wiki/New Hartford, NY (4631 Commercial Dr).html" >}}), 2006)

Acquirement

Employees could receive this pin if they filled any position within the first week of a store's opening.
