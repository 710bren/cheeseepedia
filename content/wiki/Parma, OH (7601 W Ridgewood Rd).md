The Parma OH location (Store 101) opened on November 26th 1999 in the plaza for Parmatown Mall. It opened with a Studio C Alpha, which it still has today. It remained largely unchanged until 2015, when Parmatown Mall and it's plaza was redeveloped, which led to the store's original exterior being removed.
