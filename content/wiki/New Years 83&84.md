<b><i>New Years 83&84</b></i> is a lost showtape mentioned only in the Creative Engineering showtape archive lists, released in Winter of 1983.<ref name=':0'>https://archive.org/details/ceireellists/167251805_913741462772471_2472940779602503656_n.jpg</ref>

The naming of the tape does not imply it could be agnostically used for 1983 and 1984, as [RAE New Years Eve '82]({{< ref "wiki/RAE New Years Eve '82.md" >}}) is also listed under 'New Years 82&83'.<ref name=':0' />
