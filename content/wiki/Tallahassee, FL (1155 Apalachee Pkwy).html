Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre that opened on August 9, 1982, and closed on November 18, 1984, with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. This is not to be confused with [Tallahassee, FL (2810 Sharer Rd)]({{< ref "wiki/Tallahassee, FL (2810 Sharer Rd).html" >}}), which opened on April 7, 1992, and is still open today.

Stage

Tallahassee opened with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. Barely any info is known, and after closure in November 1984, the bots were auctioned.

History

Tallahassee opened on August 9, 1982, with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. Nothing is known about Tallahassee's Pizza Time Theatre, however. The location would close on November 18, 1984, due to underperformance.

=Post Pizza Time Theatre

= The location would sit vacant for a period of time before becoming part of a Walgreen's. The Walgreen's would close between 2008 and 2010 and become Dollar Tree. As of August 2023, Dollar Tree sits on the space.
