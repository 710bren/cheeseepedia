**_Tu Eres La Estrella En Chuck E. Cheese's**_ is a cassette tape released in the mid-1990's and was only able to be purchased at the [Santiago (Mall Plaza Vespucio), Chile]({{< ref "wiki/Santiago (Mall Plaza Vespucio), Chile.md" >}}) and [Santiago (Las Condes/Lo Barnechea), Chile]({{< ref "wiki/Santiago (Las CondesLo Barnechea), Chile.md" >}}) locations. The cassette featured Chuck E. Live shows from the 1990's dubbed in Spanish. 

## Songs ##
It is currently unknown how many songs were featured on this tape. Birthday Star 1992 and Chuck E. Shuffle are the only current known songs featured on this tape.
