<b><i>Tu Eres La Estrella En Chuck E. Cheese's</b></i> (You Are The Star At Chuck E. Cheese's) is a cassette tape released in the mid-1990's and was only able to be purchased at the Santiago (Mall Plaza Vespucio), Chile and [Santiago (Las Condes/Lo Barnechea), Chile]({{< ref "wiki/Santiago (Las CondesLo Barnechea), Chile.md" >}}) locations.  

The cassette featured Chuck E. Live shows from the 1990's dubbed in Spanish. 

<h2> Playlist </h2>
It is currently unknown how many songs were featured on this tape. Birthday Star 1992 and Chuck E. Shuffle are the only current known songs featured on this tape.

* [Birthday Star '92]({{< ref "wiki/Birthday Star '92.md" >}})
* [Chuck E. Shuffle]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Shuffle.md" >}})
