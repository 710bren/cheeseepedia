**_Santa Rosa, CA (545 Ross St)**_ was a [Pizza Time Theatre]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.md" >}}) that was open from April 1981 to April 27, 1986.

##History##
The location had a unique _Sandwich Fixins Bar_ at its location.<ref>https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-press-democrat/50027079/</ref>
