**5270 Randolph Road** is a [Chuck E. Cheese's]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese's.md" >}}) that opened on August 20, 2002 with a [Studio C Beta, 32M]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.md" >}}), which is still open to this day

Rockville is the last Studio C Beta, 32M in Maryland, and will eventually be relocating to [Kensington (5238 Nicholson Ln)]({{< ref "wiki/Kensington, MD (5238 Nicholson Ln).md" >}}) sometime in 2023.
