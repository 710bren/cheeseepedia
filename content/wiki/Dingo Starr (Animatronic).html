Design

Dingo is a dog that plays the drums. He wore a jacket with varying colors depending on location, black turtle neck, and sometimes a necklace around his neck.

  • Head left
  • Head right
  • Head up
  • Left eyelid raise
  • Right eyelid raise
  • Left ear
  • Right ear
  • Left arm
  • Right arm
  • Body lean
