<b>Best of CEC TV 2003</b> released January 2003 and ran until April 2003. This was also used as a generic show which was valid from January 2003 - September 2008.

This show is made up of songs and intermissions reused from various 2000 - 2002 showtapes.

<h2> Trivia: </h2>
*Most of the songs and intermissions in this show are reused. 
**'The Muffin Man' is reused from [Just for Kids Show 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Just for Kids Show 2000.md" >}}). 
**'Wildlife' is reused from [Summertime 2000]({{< ref "wiki/Summertime 2000.md" >}}). 
**'Chuck E.', 'Drinkin' A Coca-Cola', 'Jump', and 'Closing Time?' are reused from [September 2000 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2000 Show.md" >}}). 
**'Larger Than Life', 'Baking With Betty', and 'WFW Babysitter' are reused from [January 2001 Show]({{< ref "wiki/January 2001 Show.md" >}}). 
**'Humpty Dumpty' and 'Super Jasper' are reused from [Chuck E's Rockin' Rhymes]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E's Rockin' Rhymes.md" >}}). 
**'Paper or Plastic', 'Amphicar', and 'Eddie Coker' are reused from [September 2001 Show]({{< ref "wiki/September 2001 Show.md" >}}). Also featured are bloopers of the 'Sing-along Guys' intermission from that show.
**'Tube Explorer' and 'Pasqually's Polka Minute' are reused from January 2002.
**'CEC Chuck E. Cheese', 'That's A Deal' and 'Monkey Ninjas' are reused from [April 2002 Show]({{< ref "wiki/April 2002 Show.md" >}}).
*All of the puppet footage for Studio C is reused from each song's original show.
*'The Muffin Man' does not feature programming or Studio C puppets in this show.
*Chuck's curtains stay closed during 'Chuck E.' on all versions of the show.
