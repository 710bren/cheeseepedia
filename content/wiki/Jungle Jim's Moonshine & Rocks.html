Some of those being Moonshine jugs, Rocks & Rolfe's Rainbow. These were all located at Jungle Jim's International Market in Cincinnati, Ohio. (5440 Dixie Hwy, Fairfield, OH 45014) at one time.

Moonshine Jug History

In 1988 the moonshine jugs were installed on }}">Elvis (The King Retrofit) stagehttps:imgur.com/a/ojDIOE6 these rocks would remain on the stage until 1998 when the stage would receive a make over removing the Moonshine Jugs from the stage.(1) They would then be relocated to the area above the 'Lowest Prices on Premium Cigars' sign.(2) They would stay in that area until 2005 when Jungle Jim's would expand with the display from the Premium Cigars area being relocated to above the cheese aisle(3). Then in 2018 the display originally from the Premium Cigars section was trashed which included the Moonshine jugs ending the 30 year history.
