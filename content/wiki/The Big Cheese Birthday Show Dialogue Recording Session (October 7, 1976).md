The **_Big Cheese Birthday Show Dialogue Recording Session**_ was a 2-track tape containing a showtape concept demo recording produced on October 7, 1976.<ref name=':0'>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C2cQFyz12hA</ref> 

The tape features 'The Big Cheese' (whom later on became [Chuck E. Cheese]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese.md" >}})), and Crusty the Cat performing dialogue.

## Production ##
The audio would be produced at _Wally Heider Studio_ (245 Hyde St., San Francisco, CA, 94102). The script for the session was written by Robert “Bob” Allan Black under the direction of Atari, Gene Landrum, and Nolan Bushnell. The producer for the show would be _Ed Bogus Productions_ (680 Beach St. Suite 495, San Francisco, CA 94109).<ref>https://archive.org/details/sanfranciscosanf1978rlpo/page/n373/mode/2up?q=%22Bogas+Productions%22</ref><ref name=':0' />
