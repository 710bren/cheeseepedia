**_The Flag Wavers**_ _were_ a 'retrofit' of [Flag Wavers (Cyberamic)]({{< ref "wiki/Flag Wavers (Cyberamic).md" >}}) at _Jungle Jim's International Market_ in Cincinnati, Ohio. (5440 Dixie Hwy, Fairfield, OH 45014)

The Flag Wavers were installed in 1988 during the 1988 Expansion of Jungle Jim's. The last original flag wavers were removed in 2018. 

## History ##
The Flag Wavers originally installed in 1988 where [Elvis (The King Retrofit)]({{< ref "wiki/Elvis (The King Retrofit).md" >}}) was originally installed. Flags were installed to the left and right of his stage area. After the stage moved in 2001 the flags were relocated near check out. In 2018 the final real Flag Wavers were removed with a scar where they once were installed being the only remains. 

In 2005 Jungle Jim's made reproduction Flag Wavers which would be located to the right of the Big Cheese.
