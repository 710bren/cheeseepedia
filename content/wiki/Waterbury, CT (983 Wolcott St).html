+++ title = "Waterbury, CT (983 Wolcott St)" draft = false tags = ["Store List"] date = "2024-04-25" [Article] startDate = "1982-4-00" endDate = "1984-00-00" contributors = ["2600:6C65:7B7F:DF05:7977:4E85:9DC9:71FB","Cheeseclassic","Ryys123","CoolerDude"] +++ Waterbury, CT (983 Wolcott St) was a Pizza Time Theatre that opened in April 1982 and closed in 1984. It was at auction before everything was thrown out. This location is not to be confused with the [Waterbury, CT (650 Wolcott St)]({{< ref "wiki/Waterbury, CT (650 Wolcott St).html" >}}) location, which opened on September 29, 1992, and closed on June 25, 2020. This location was the first in the New England region. It was located in the 2 Brothers Plaza. It had a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]], with only a few photos of it. As of March 2024, the location is a Goodwill Waterbury Store & Donation Station.
