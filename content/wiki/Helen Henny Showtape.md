|pttreel=y}}The **_Helen Henny Showtape**_ is a currently lost _Pizza Time Theatre_ showtape that debuted at the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) in August of 1977.

## History ##
The confirmation of this showtapes existence is from the installation of  at the Winchester location on August 12, 1977.<ref>https://archive.org/details/atariincbusiness0000gold/page/256/mode/2up</ref> The showtape would run until [Madame Oink I]({{< ref "wiki/Madame Oink I.md" >}}) replaced it in February of 1978.
