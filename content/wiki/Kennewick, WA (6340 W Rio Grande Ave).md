<b><i>6340 W Rio Grande Ave</b></i> is a Chuck E. Cheese that opened on October 1, 2018 and is still open today with a Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor) (Dance Floor).

This was the relocation of the [Richland, WA (2610 N Columbia Center Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/Richland, WA (2610 N Columbia Center Blvd).md" >}}) store, which opened on September 30, 1982 with a [Balcony Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Balcony Stage.md" >}}) and closed on September 9, 2018 with a [Road Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Road Stage.md" >}}).
