Design

This stage kept the same concept as the Balcony Stage; however, it was now themed to be a house-like setting. Behind four of the characters sat horizontal siding with windows with lighting behind a pane. The base of which the characters and windows sat had a wall of horizontal siding or railing in front of the original Balcony Stage front with lattice directly under it. The faux valence of the stage became a shingled roof with popcorn lights above Munch, Chuck E., & Pasqually. Some installations had a live stage instead of the lattice (some had the distinct 'C', and some didn't). It featured checkered flooring, fencing, and steps to get up.
