Stage

Abilene opened with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. Barely info is known, and the bots were auctioned shortly after closure. {| class='wikitable' |+ !Stage !Installed !Removed |- |Balcony Stage |November 1981 |1984 |}

History

Abilene opened on November 23, 1981, with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. It was located on the former Kmart Foods space, which closed in 1979/1980. Other than this, there is nothing known about this store at all. The store closed in 1984 (month and day unknown) due to underperformance. The building was left vacant for a long period of time until the mid 2010s, when it was redeveloped.
