<b><i>San Salvador, ES (503 Carretera Panamericana)</b></i>  is a <i>Chuck E. Cheese</i> that opened on April 13, 2021 in the Multiplaza Mall in the country’s capital city, San Salvador.

<h2> History </h2>

<h2>= Background </h2>=
Continuing they’re international expansion, most notably in Latin America, Chuck E. Cheese decided to open a location in San Salvador, ES. in the Multiplaza mall. The store is located on the second floor of the mall.

<h2>= Opening </h2>=
The location opened on April 13, 2021. The location received press from PR Newswire.<blockquote>'Chuck E. Cheese is thrilled to bring our signature fun and games to the El Salvador community, expanding deeper into Central America is an exciting step for the brand, as part of a larger international plan to bring a safe and fun environment 'where a kid can be a kid' to families worldwide.'

- Arun Barnes, COO and SVP of International, Chuck E. Cheese.</blockquote>The location received positive reviews from Salvadorans.
