**_1919 South Reed Rd**_ is a Chuck E. Cheese's location that opened in Feburary 2004 with a [[Small-Town Studio C]] stage, and are still open to this day.

##  History ## 
Kokomo would open in Feburary 2004, right across from the Kokomo Town Shopping Center. They opened as a [[Phase 3]] remodel with many Phase 3 features, including posters and games. It is also one of the 4 Small-Town Studio C stages in the state of Indiana. It is also one of only 3 Phase 3 locations in Kentucky, all opening in 2004. As of Feburary 2023, Kokomo still has the Small-Town Studio C, and has not remodeled.

##  Stages ## 
Originally, when the store opened, they opened with a [[Small-Town Studio C]], which they still have to this day.
