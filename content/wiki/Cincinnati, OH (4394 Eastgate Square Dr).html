History

Eastgate opened on June 17, 1990 it was a franchise store owned by 'Bernstein Restaurant Group' who also owned }}">Florence, KY (7635 Mall Rd). The store opened with the }}">SPT 1988 Remodel Program and }}">Chuck E.’s House.
