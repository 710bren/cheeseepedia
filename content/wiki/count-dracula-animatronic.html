History

Dracula was created in 1976 for Castle Dracula in Wildwood, New Jersey, but he was destroyed in a fire on January 16th, 2002, and even before the fire, he was in disrepair. (1)

Design

This animatronic has a stark resemblances to the Bela Lugosi Dracula from Universal Pictures' Dracula movies.

Movements

The animatronic seems to have 6 movements, he was also on a metal track that transported him outside. (2)
