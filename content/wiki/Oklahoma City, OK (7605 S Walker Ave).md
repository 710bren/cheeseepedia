**'7605 S Walker Ave ,**_ was a _Showbiz Pizza Place/Chuck E. Cheese's_ that opened on March 18th, 1981 with a Rock-afire Explosion, which closed and was demolished for the [current location]({{< ref "wiki/Oklahoma City, OK (7601 S Walker Ave).md" >}}) in 2005 (month and day unknown), as a Chuck E. Cheese's with a 3-Stage. The new location opened in May 2005 and is still open with 2.0.
