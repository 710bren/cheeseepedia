The **_Beach Bear Diagnostic Tape**_ is a 1981 [[Rock-afire Explosion]] diagnostic [[showtape]] that used the [[CEI Betamax]] format to display video for reference in adjustments.<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gjiR5vrAUCU&pp=ygUYc2hvd2JpeiBkaWFnbm9zdGljcyAxOTgx</ref>

The tape was part of a diagnostic package also containing the [[Billy Bob Diagnostic Tape]] and [[Rolfe & Earl Diagnostic Tape]].
