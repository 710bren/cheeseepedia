## Trivia: ##
The filming location for Showbiz Pizza was the Arlington, TX (2216 S Fielder Rd) location.

This show was Nancy Lenihan final show as Helen Henny.

Final showtape for the Cyberstar Intro & It's Showtime (Video Breaks) as it gets replaced with CEC-TV for the following shows.

Chuck E's Place would be reused in the future starting with the live show Land of 1000 Dances 1991, and most famously Havin' A Party in 1992.
