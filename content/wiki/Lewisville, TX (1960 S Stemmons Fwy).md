**_Lewisville, TX (1960 S Stemmons Fwy)**_ was a [TJ Hartford's Sports Grille]({{< ref "wiki/TJ Hartford's Sports Grille.md" >}}) that opened on October 1, 2001, and closed on February 17, 2008.

The location was the only one of its kind, though a second location had planned to be opened in 2002.<ref>https://www.annualreports.com/HostedData/AnnualReportArchive/c/NYSE_CEC_2001.pdf</ref>
##History##
The location would open on October 1, 2001<ref name=':0'>https://web.archive.org/web/20020605030127fw_/http://www.tjhartfords.com/company.html</ref>, though another source also states September 2001- possibly a soft opening.<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20020209004806/http://dfw.citysearch.com/E/V/DALTX/0233/22/17/cs1.html</ref> By April of 2003 the store's signage and products would change logos to reflect a rebrand in iconography and name.<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20041129040954/http://tjhartfords.com/</ref>
###Closure###
The store would close on February 17, 2008.<ref name=':02'>https://web.archive.org/web/20020605030127fw_/http://www.tjhartfords.com/company.html</ref> The location, its equipment, and its land would sell in April of 2008 for $1.3 million. In connection with the sale, a $0.8 million gain was also had with 'Other operating expenses'.<ref>https://www.annualreports.com/HostedData/AnnualReportArchive/c/NYSE_CEC_2008.pdf</ref> The store would be replaced in the following months with a Hooters.
