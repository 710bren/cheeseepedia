Stages

Originally when the store opened, they had a Studio C Prototype, this was a rare stage, being installed in only 5 locations, with Clarksville being the last to be installed. They were the first Prototype to get the 2.0 Remodel in August / September 2019, being replaced with the Dance Floor.
