+++ title = "Parkway Parade, Marine Parade, Singapore" draft = false tags = ["Store List"] date = "2023-11-03" [Article] startDate = "1983-12-15" endDate = "1985-00-00" contributors = ["CorrectorMan","Rexburg090","BurgersNuggs445","Tux Chuck"] +++ Parkway Parade, Marine Parade, Singapore was a Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza Time Theatre that opened on December 15, 1983, and closed around 1985 or 1986 (date unknown) with a [[Cyberamics|Balcony Stage]]. This was the first and only Pizza Time Theatre to operate in Singapore. This Pizza Time Theatre was under the same franchise as the China locations.

Stage

The location opened with the standard Balcony Stage. After closure, the show was sent to the franchisee's separate entertainment centerhttps://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PI8X8ILOoqg

History

This location opened on December 15, 1983 in the Parkway Parade Shopping Mall. There is little documentation on this location, other than prints marking its opening or advertisements. The location closed in the mid 80s for unknown reasons, might be due to being an underperforming store or general parking problems. The location was later split up into multiple businesses. A little known fact about the location is that it also featured [Cheese E. Street]({{< ref "wiki/Cheese E Street.html" >}}).
