Cheese that opened on December 23rd, 2003 with a [[Studio C Beta | Studio C Beta, 16M]], and is still open to this day with the [[CEC 2018 2.0 Remodel Program|2.0 Remodel]].

History

=Early Years

= Superior opened in the Superior Marketplace on December 23, 2003 with a [[Studio C Beta|Studio C Beta, 16M]]. For unknown reasons, Superior never had a road sign. Not much is known about Superior in the early years, besides the removal of SkyTubes and the Toddler Zone in October 2020. Currently, there is no footage of Superior in the 2000's.

=2.0 Remodel

= Superior was closed for the [[CEC 2018 2.0 Remodel Program|2.0 Remodel]]. The Studio C Beta was removed in favor of a [[Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor)|Dance Floor]] and a [Video Wall]({{< ref "wiki/Video Wall.html" >}}). The Grand Reopening Event took place on May 5th, 2022. As of September 2023, Superior still has the 2.0 Remodel.
