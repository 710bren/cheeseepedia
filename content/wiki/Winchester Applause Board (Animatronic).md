The <b><i>Winchester Applause Board</b></i> was an animatronic created by [Fantasy Forest]({{< ref "wiki/Fantasy Forest.md" >}}) in 1977 for the first <i>Pizza Time Theatre</i> on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}})<ref>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf</ref>. the bot was programmed using the [Winchester Hardware Setup]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Hardware Setup.md" >}}) & Controlled using the [Winchester Control System]({{< ref "wiki/Winchester Control System.md" >}}).

The Bot was removed when Winchester closed in 1985 & sent to the [Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office.md" >}}) where it remained until the move to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.md" >}}).

<h2> History </h2>
The bot was produced in 1977 by Fantasy Forest. The bot was put in the Pizza Time Theatre on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) somewhere in 1977. The bot was at Winchester until 1985, when it was removed & most likely sent to the [Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office.md" >}}). The bot was most likely thrown out when PTT was moving to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.md" >}}).

<h2> Design </h2>
Not much is known about the design of the bot. The bot had a Wood barrier around several clapping animal arms.
