**_2803 Brewerton Rd**_, was a Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza Time Theater that opened in August 1983 (day unknown), with a [\2](\1) and closed in November 1996 (day unknown), as a Chuck E. Cheese's with a [[2-Stage]].

This location should not be confused with the [\2](\1) location, which opened on December 27th, 2002 with a [[Studio C Beta]], and is still open to this day with the 2.0 remodel.
