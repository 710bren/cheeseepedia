**_509 Illinois Route 59**_ is a _Chuck E. Cheese_ that opened on March 9, 2015 as the relocation of the  location which opened on April 4th, 1983 with a Rock-Afire Explosion and closed in March 1st, 2015 with a Studio C Alpha.
##History##
Naperville opened its doors on March 9th 2015 with the 2015 Remodel and Circles of Light stage. Some games were from the old Naperville location and as of March 2023, the location still has the 2015 remodel and Circles of Lights stage.
##Stages##
Naperville opened with the Circles of Lights stage which they still have to this day.
