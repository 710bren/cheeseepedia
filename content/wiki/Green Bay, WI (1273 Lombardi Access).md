**1273 Lombardi Access** was a Showbiz Pizza Place that opened on October 28, 1982, which is still open to this day.

This location received Concept Unification somewhere in 1991, then received one of the first Studio C Beta, 32m's in the Fall of 2000, then received 2.0 in September 2021.

## History ##
Green Bay opened as the second Showbiz Pizza Place in Wisconsin, after Madison which opened 3 months prior, they originally feature the Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations did. they would have it for a few years, until around 1991, when they would undergo a process known as Concept Unification, where the RAE characters were changed to the Chuck E. Cheese characters. They would have this stage for the next 9-10 years, until around Fall of 2000, when they would replace the 3-Stage with a Studio C Beta, 32m, they were actually one of the first ones, and featured gold lettering on the 'CEC' part, unlike later Betas, where they would be colored red. they would have this stage for the next 20-21 years or so, until September of 2021, when they would receive the 2.0 remodel, this involved removing the animatronic stage and replacing it with a  and changing the whole place. as of September 2022, they still have this remodel.

## Stages ##
Originally, Green Bay featured a Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations did, in around 1991, they would undergo Concept Unification, turning the RAE into the 3-Stage, they would have the 3-Stage until around Fall of 2000, when they would receive one of the first Studio C Betas, this featured a 32m Chuck E. animatronic, like what was seen on the Studio C Alphas, in the mid 2000's, they would have the Avenger outfit, until around 2013 or 2014, when they would put the Rockstar jeans on Chuck E., Green Bay was one of the first to have it, they would keep the Avenger shirt until they removed the stage. In September 2021, they would replace the Beta with a Dance Floor and Video Wall, as part of their 2.0 remodel, they were the only Studio C Beta in Wisconsin. As of September 2022, they still have this remodel.
