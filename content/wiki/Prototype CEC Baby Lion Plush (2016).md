The <b><i>Prototype CEC Baby Lion Plush</b></i> was an unreleased plush created as a prototype on April 6, 2016.
<h2>Design</h2>
The design is a beige lion with a muted rainbow mane. Its paws are brown with hearts for paw pads. Its chest has an embroidered Rockstar era Chuck E. Cheese logo. The tag has additional info.
