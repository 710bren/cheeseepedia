The **_Prototype CEC Baby Lion Plush**_ was an unreleased plush created as a prototype on April 6, 2016.
##Design##
The design is a beige lion with a muted rainbow mane. Its paws are brown with hearts for paw pads. Its chest has an embroidered Rockstar era Chuck E. Cheese logo. The tag has additional info.
 DFI #: FH14761-1 4/6/16
 PLUSH BABY LION WITH EMBROIDERED CEC HEAD ON BODY
 CEC FOB: $ 3.79EA
