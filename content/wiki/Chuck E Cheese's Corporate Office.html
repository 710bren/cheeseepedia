Cheese's Corporate Office is the current headquarters used by CEC Entertainment. They moved into the building in July 2015, after moving from the old [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.html" >}}). The building is located at 1707 Market Pl Blvd #200, Irving, TX 75063, in the Lennar DFW Division Office.
