Stages

Originally, the store opened with a [[Studio C Beta|Studio C Beta, 16m]], they were the 4th location to open with a 16m Beta. they would have this for a couple years, until around 2018 or 2019, when they would undergo the 2.0 remodel, they would remove the stage and replace with the 2017 Model of the [[Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor)|Dance Floor]].
