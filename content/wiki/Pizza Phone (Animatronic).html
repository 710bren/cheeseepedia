It was only used on the [Studio C Alpha]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Alpha.md" >}}) stage, and was also used at [Jeddah, Saudi Arabia (Corniche Road)]({{< ref "wiki/Jeddah (Corniche Road), Saudi Arabia.md" >}}) on their unique [Studio C Beta Prototype]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta Prototype.md" >}}).

History

The Pizza Phone was created by Garner Holt Productions in 1997 as part of the [Studio C Alpha]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Alpha.md" >}}) stage, situated next to Chuck in the main booth. Pizza Phones would be installed from 1997-2002, alongside 2012 and 2015. In December of 2001, it would be installed in the [Jeddah, Saudi Arabia (Corniche Road)]({{< ref "wiki/Jeddah (Corniche Road), Saudi Arabia.md" >}}) location on their unique [Studio C Beta Prototype]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta Prototype.md" >}}). The phone would not be included in the designs for [Studio C Beta]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.md" >}}) and [Studio C Cappa]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Cappa.md" >}}) going forward. In July of 2019 the Jeddah store would close, leaving the rest of Pizza Phones exclusively at [Studio C Alpha]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Alpha.md" >}}) locations. As of August 2024, only 7 Pizza Phones remain in stores.

Design

The Pizza Phone is designed like a slice of pizza, except it doubles as a telephone, complete with a phone on top, a cord on the side, a dial in the middle, which also has a 'C' in the middle of it. there are also lights inside, 3 for the pizza part, and 2 inside the dial, and the full figure swings out from the wall. The figure is hung on the wall to Chuck's right, at his eye level.
