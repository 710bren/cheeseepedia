## Trivia: ##

* 'Let's Have A Party' is originally from Chuck E. Cheese's Show 1 2016 (Show 1 2016), 'Dream Big' is originally from Chuck E. Cheese's Show 5 2016 (Show 5 2016), 'It's A Date' was originally from Chuck E. Cheese's Show 5 2015 (Show 5 2015,) 'Happy To Be Seeing You' is originally from [Summer 2014]({{< ref "wiki/July 2014 Show.md" >}}).
* 'CEC Sketch - 'Where's Steve Waters?' is reused from Act 1 of [Summer 2014]({{< ref "wiki/July 2014 Show.md" >}}) but they cut it to start after Hard Hat Hanks scene. 
* 'Happy To Be Seeing You' was reprogrammed for Studio C.
* Many of the songs and intermissions were reused from various shows from 2016.
