Stage

San Jose/Cupertino opened with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. This particular stage never received a Helen Henny, as they closed before the Summer 1983 showtape. However, there is basically no documentation, and after closure, their fate is currently unknown. {| class='wikitable' |+ !Stage !Installed !Removed !Notes |- |[[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]] |1982 |May 1983 |N/A |}

History

San Jose/Cupertino opened in 1982 (month and day unknown) with a [[Cyberamics Balcony Stage|Balcony Stage]]. Unfortunately, there is basically no documentation on this store anywhere. The location would close in May 1983 (day unknown) for unknown reasons, but likely due to it underperforming or their lease expiring. As of August 2023, the location is now part of a Home Depot.
