+++ title = "Lewisville, TX (1960 S Stemmons Fwy)" draft = false tags = ["Store List"] date = "2023-09-22" [Article] startDate = "2001-10-1" endDate = "2008-2-17" pageThumbnailFile = "TJ Hartfords Sports Grille Thumb.png|Store Exterior" contributors = ["The 64th Gamer","SinStar"] +++ Lewisville, TX (1960 S Stemmons Fwy) was a [TJ Hartford's Sports Grille]({{< ref "wiki/TJ Hartford's Sports Grille.md" >}}) that opened on October 1, 2001, and closed on February 17, 2008. The location was the only one of its kind, though a second location had planned to be opened in 2002.https://www.annualreports.com/HostedData/AnnualReportArchive/c/NYSE_CEC_2001.pdf

History

The location would open on October 1, 2001https:web.archive.org/web/20020605030127fw_/http:www.tjhartfords.com/company.html, though another source also states September 2001- possibly a soft opening.https:web.archive.org/web/20020209004806/http:dfw.citysearch.com/E/V/DALTX/0233/22/17/cs1.html By April of 2003 the store's signage and products would change logos to reflect a rebrand in iconography and name.https:web.archive.org/web/20041129040954/http:tjhartfords.com/

=Closure

= The store would close on February 17, 2008.https:web.archive.org/web/20020605030127fw_/http:www.tjhartfords.com/company.html The location, its equipment, and its land would sell in April of 2008 for $1.3 million. In connection with the sale, a $0.8 million gain was also had with 'Other operating expenses'.https://www.annualreports.com/HostedData/AnnualReportArchive/c/NYSE_CEC_2008.pdf The store would be replaced in the following months with a Hooters.
