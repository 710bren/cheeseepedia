##History##
Butler opened on April 21st, 2004 in the Clearview Mall (Alongside the still operational [Wilkes-Barre Township, PA]({{< ref "wiki/Wilkes-Barre Township, PA (2120 Market Place).md" >}}) location) with the Small Town Studio C & the Cool Chuck outfit. The store would remain unmodeled until they closed. Sometime after closure, the location was converted into Dollar Tree. As of April 2023, Dollar Tree is still there.
##Stages##
The store opened on April 21st, 2004 with the Small-Town Studio C & the Cool Chuck outfit. It was one of 4 installed in Pennsylvania. The store would have their Small Town Studio C. It is unknown if Chuck E ever had any other outfits than Cool Chuck.
