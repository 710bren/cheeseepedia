The <b><i>Winchester Flag Wavers</b></i> was an animatronic produced by [Fantasy Forest]({{< ref "wiki/Fantasy Forest.md" >}}) for the [Pizza Time Theatre]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre.md" >}}) on [San Jose, Ca (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).md" >}}) in May 1977. The Bot was a collection of waving flags that were around the room that were controlled through one animatronic system.

The Bot was used from May 1977 up until either Winchester got [Cyberamics]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics.md" >}}) in 1981, or when the location closed in 1985.

<h2> History </h2>
The Winchester Flag Wavers were installed in May 1977 in the dining area<ref name=':0'>https:<i>www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf (Contains the Thumbnail image & Broad Dining Area Shot.)</ref>. They would wave along to the showtapes<ref>https:</i>www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_77menu.pdf</ref>. The Bot appeared in the 'The Great All-American Pizza Show' Brochure in 1979, where there referred to as just 'flags'<ref name=':0' />. The Bot would remain at Winchester until it got Cyberamics in 1981. The Bot's were either sent to [Sunnyvale]({{< ref "wiki/Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office.md" >}}), or thrown out.
