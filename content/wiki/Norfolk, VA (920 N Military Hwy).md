**_920 Military Hwy Norfolk, VA**_ was a _Chuck E. Cheese's_ that opened in 2000 with a [\2](\1), which closed on May 28th, 2015.

## Stages## 
This location featured the standard [\2](\1)  installed in locations at the time. This location kept the interactive console till closure.

## History##
