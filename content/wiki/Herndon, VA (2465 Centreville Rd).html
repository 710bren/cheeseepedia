History

Herndon opened on May 19, 1992. At first, they had a [2-Stage]({{< ref "wiki/2-Stage.html" >}}). Nothing special is known about it, and stayed up until the location received the [CEC 2005 Remodel Program]({{< ref "wiki/CEC 2005 Remodel Program.html" >}}) in 2007, replacing the [2-Stage]({{< ref "wiki/2-Stage.html" >}}) with a [Studio C Beta]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.html" >}}). It would remain this way until July 2023, when the location received the [CEC 2018 2.0 Remodel Program]({{< ref "wiki/CEC 2018 20 Remodel Program.html" >}}), meaning the Studio C was removed for a [Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor)]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor).html" >}}) + [Video Wall]({{< ref "wiki/Video Wall.html" >}}). The location also received a Trampoline Zone.
