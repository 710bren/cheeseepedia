**_7401 Good Hope Rd**_ was a _Showbiz Pizza Place_/_Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza_ that opened on June 17th, 1981 as a Franchised Showbiz Pizza store with a Rock-afire Explosion, the store closed in 1995 (Unknown Month and Day) with a 1-Stage. The store was located next to Kmart (Which closed in 2003) in the former Kmart Foods space.

##  History ## 
Milwaukee (Good Hope Rd) opened on June 17th, 1981 as the first Showbiz Pizza Place in Wisconsin as a Franchised store in the space that once housed a Kmart Foods Grocery Store. Milwaukee (Good Hope Rd) originally featured the Rock-afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations did. they would have it for a few years, until 1988, when it was removed and sent to [\2](\1) for the opening on their location in June (It would then undergo Concept Unification around 3 years later). It would then be replaced with [\2](\1) (Which got it after they reopened) and [\2](\1). They would then have it until sometime in 1994 when they received the [[1994 SPT Repositioning / Remodel Program]] which involved the conversion of [[Chuck E.'s House]] to a 1-Stage, they then would remain this way until the store was permanently closed a year later in 1995 for unknown reasons. The whole building, including the neighboring Kmart would then be demolished for a Home Depot for 2005, which closed in 2008. The site is now a Pick 'n Save. As of May 2023, Pick 'n Save is still in the building. 

##  Stages ## 
Originally, Milwaukee (Good Hope Rd) featured a Rock-afire Explosion, as all other Showbiz locations did. In May/June 1988, they would remove the Rock-afire Explosion (They would then be sent to Raleigh for their opening in June 1988 and converted to CEC 3 years later in around 1991) for Chuck E.'s House. They would then convert Chuck E.'s House into a 1-Stage. They had the 1-Stage until the store's closure sometime in 1995 (Unknown Month and Day) and the show would be possibily be sent to [[Bellevue, WA (2239 148th Ave)]] for the opening of their location 1996 where it still operates as of May 2023.
