**R City Mall, 1st Floor, Lal Bahadur Shastri Rd, next to Pantaloons (R City Mall, Mumbai, Maharastra)** is a Chuck E. Cheese that opened on December 20, 2019 with a Dance Floor, which is still open to this day.

This location is the very first location in the country of India, and was also the last store to open in 2019, and the 2010's decade.
