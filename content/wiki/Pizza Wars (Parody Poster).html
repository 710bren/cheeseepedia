Production

This poster was one of the few Rock-Afire Explosion parody posters released in 1988. Once [Concept Unification]({{< ref "wiki/Concept Unification.html" >}}) began this poster would cease production in 1990. This poster has been re-released by Creative Engineering after Concept Unification after mass fanfare for the Rock-afire Explosion has been ignited in the 2000s.https://creativeengineering.bigcartel.com/

Design

The poster is a parody of the [https:en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars_(film) Star Wars] movie poster from 1977.https:filmartgallery.com/products/star-wars-9859 This piece details Duke LaRue as Luke Skywalker, Mitzi Mozzarella as Princess Leia, Billy Bob and Beach Bear as C-3PO and R2-D2. As completely new art was created for the characters, Billy Bob utilizes a stock pose.https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/spp/spp_print-ad-artwork.pdf
