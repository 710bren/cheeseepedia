History

Peek Trail opened in early 2004 (month and day unknown) with the [[Phase 3 (2000)|Phase 3]] remodel, which included some late 1990s-early 2000s decor, [Skytubes]({{< ref "wiki/Skytubes.html" >}}), and a [[Studio C 2000|Studio C Beta]] with a 16M bot. This location did not receive the 2005 remodel at any point and was untouched throughout the years. By the late 2010s, the Skytubes were removed. This location was also the very last location to have wall planters. Around August 2022, Peek Trail received the 2.0 remodel, removing the Beta after 18 years and a 2021 Model Dance Floor and Video Wall took its place. As of July 2023, Peek Trail still has the 2.0 remodel.
