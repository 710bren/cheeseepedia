<h2>= 2024 </h2>=

* <b>August 17th -</b> All CSS is moved out of Common.css to allow per-theme modifications.
* <b>August 1st -</b> Featured Articles return to the main page.
* <b>July 15th -</b> New homepage layout is implemented.
* <b>July 14th -</b> Testing for a new homepage begins.
* <b>June 22nd -</b> Removed the very old 'Terms & Additional Topics' page, moving terms to :Category:Animatronic Repair & Preservation, and deleting the disambiguation pages.
* <b>May 31st -</b> The site is mentioned by <i>The Charlotte Ledger</i> in a news article.<ref>https://charlotteledger.substack.com/p/in-a-chuck-e-cheese-on-pineville</ref>
* <b>May 26th</b> <b>-</b> New admin <i>Stripes</i> fixes many CSS problems with the wiki for a better look. CirrusSearch, AdvancedSearch, and RelatedArticles extensions enabled by Miraheze Stewards.
* <b>May 24th -</b> The wiki hits 1500 articles.
* <b>May 22nd -</b> <nowiki>''</nowiki>Advanced Animations', 'AVG Technologies', 'VP Animations', 'Hofmann Figuren', and 'Unknown Manufacturers' sections begin being worked on.
* <b>May 2nd-</b> Creative Presentations section added to homepage
* <b>April 29th -</b> Browser extension <i>Indie Wiki Buddy</i> adds CEP as the overwriting site for the CEC Fandom Wiki.
* <b>January 24th -</b> 'Creative Presentations' sections begin being worked on.

<h2>= 2023 </h2>=
* <b>December 18th -</b> Added auto-updating ranking of top 5 users in past 30 days to the main page. 
* <b>November 27th -</b> 'Sally Corp' section added to the front page tabs.
* <b>November 2nd -</b> The wiki's front page changes to have a 'Home' tab instead of information being under the logo.
* <b>October 23rd -</b> Miraheze updates the wiki to Mediawiki 1.40, moving all sidebars to hidden menus and decluttering the pages.
* <b>July 8th -</b> The wiki begins its first community project for contributing to specific wiki sections: :Category:Store Fixtures#Chuck%20E.%20Cheese's-0 (Store Fixtures) of the [Artifact Shelves]({{< ref "wiki/Artifact Shelves.md" >}}) & 1990s Major Remodel
* <b>July 4th -</b> The wiki hits 1000 articles.
* <b>June 19th -</b> First non CEC/SPP/PTT/CEI article created going over [DACS]({{< ref "wiki/DACS.md" >}}), though isn't integrated into site navigation yet.
* <b>April 18th -</b>  Media Viewer and Popup extensions enabled for easier browsing of the site.
* <b>April 17th</b> <i>-</i> Community votes on wiki name change to 'Cheese-E-Pedia' to avoid trademark violations.
* <b>March 31st -</b> Removed Mobile Frontend to align layout to the PC experience for mobile users.
* <b>February 5th -</b> The site receives a massive design overhaul, cleaning up the front page, adding tabs, and setting site colors to a deep blue. Sections for FECs and retrofitted animatronic shows are also added for users to contribute to.
* <b>February 3rd -</b> The wiki reaches 700 articles.

<h2>= 2022 </h2>=

* <b>December 31st -</b> Wiki host Miraheze merges November pages back into Chuck-E-Pedia, successfully bringing back the wiki in full.
* <b>December 19th -</b> Wiki host Miraheze confirms the lost pages from November are recovered and will be implemented soon.
* <b>December 16th -</b> An incorporation of Joe Lewis' token and ticket identification standard is added to the wiki.
* <b>December 5th -</b> Infoboxes, thumbnail embeds, and other styling is set in the site's design standards.
* <b>November 30th -</b> The flower-styled icon is applied to the site design.
* <b>November 29th -</b> A backup from August 2022 is restored and the Wiki goes back online.
* <b>November 22nd -</b> The Wiki's [https://static.miraheze.org/decodedocdigitizewiki/5/5a/Pedia_Favicon.png flower-styled icon] is designed.
* <b>November 20th -</b>  The Wiki's Discord Server reaches 1000 members.
* <b>November 17th -</b> Wiki host Miraheze loses all files to the wiki, and the site goes offline.
* <b>October 7th -</b> The Wiki's complete [https://static.miraheze.org/decodedocdigitizewiki/b/bf/Chuckepedialogo.png flower-styled logo] is designed and added to the site.
* <b>September 5th -</b> A [https://static.miraheze.org/decodedocdigitizewiki/archive/b/bf/20221008003306%21Chuckepedialogo.png preliminary logo] is added to the site's design.
* <b>August 21st -</b> The Wiki's first major lost media search, that being of the 1979 Pizza Time Theatre Ad, commences.
* <b>July 14th -</b> Early opening of Wiki to the public. Site name changes finally to 'Chuck-E-Pedia'
* <b>July 10th -</b> Announcement of public unveiling on July 17th.
* <b>February 18th -</b> Wiki name changes to 'Chuck E. Encyclopedia'

<h2>= 2021 </h2>=

* <b>November 28th -</b> Wiki goes live as 'Decode-Document-Digitize Wiki'. Visibility set to private.
