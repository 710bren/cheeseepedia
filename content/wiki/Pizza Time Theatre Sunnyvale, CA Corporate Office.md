The <b><i>Pizza Time Theatre Sunnyvale, CA Corporate Office</b></i> (1213 Innsbruck Drive, Sunnyvale, California 94086) is the third and final Pizza Time Theatre office before its merger with <i>Showbiz Pizza Place</i> and the eventual move to the [Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office.md" >}}) in Irving, Texas.

The offices were originally built in 1979, with the property measuring 31,628 square feet.<ref>https://www.commercialcafe.com/commercial-property/us/ca/sunnyvale/1213-innsbruck-drive/</ref>
