+++ title = "Valve Banks" draft = false tags = ["Animatronic Repair & Preservation"] date = "2024-08-05" [Article] startDate = "" endDate = "" contributors = ["Showbizpizzawiki"] +++ Valve banks are a array of pneumatic solenoid valves. they are used to put air (or hydraulic oil in some cases) into the cylinders. The valves are electrically operated. Cyberamic Clippard valves use 24V DC and Rockafire MAC valves also use 24V DC.
