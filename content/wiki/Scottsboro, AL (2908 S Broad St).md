##Information##
Pizza Party USA was an FEC that opened in the late 90s with a Rock A-fire Explosion, it fell into disrepair only a few years after opening and was owned by a woman named Gana Garner. The original address is 2908 S Broad St
Scottsboro, AL 35769. After closure the show was sent back to CEI.
