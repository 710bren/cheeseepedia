**3146 Sports Arena Blvd** is a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened in Rosecrans Plaza on April 19th, 1993 with a 1-stage.

This location is one of the 5 CEC's in San Diego County.

## History ##
This location had the first 1-stage installed in the San Diego area, followed by La Mesa in August of the same year. They also had the 1991 record posters from opening until 2003.
