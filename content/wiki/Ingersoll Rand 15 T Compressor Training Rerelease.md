The <b><i>15T Compressor System Video Tape Rerelease</b></i> was a training tape released in the late 90's detailing how to service the [3-Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Concept Unification Stage.md" >}}) <i>Ingersoll Rand</i> compressor. It would be an exact rerelease of the [15T Compressor System Video Tape]({{< ref "wiki/15T Compressor System Video Tape.md" >}}), and the second known rerelease.
