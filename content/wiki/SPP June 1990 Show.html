SPP June 1990 Show is one of the last few showtapes released for Showbiz Pizza Place's hosting the Rock-afire Explosion.

For some stores, this was the last tape used before Concept Unification would start at their location. Each tape sent after }}">SPP Christmas '89 / Wave Bday would become increasingly more rare as stores would convert to 3-Stages.

It was also the final showtape to feature any new Rock-afire Explosion material from CEI as Friends, and Rock & Roll Tribute were the last segments they've released.

In September 1990 they released }}">SPP Best Of 1 Kids/Friends out of 5 in a series of compilation tapes, Showbiz Pizza Place locations would play them until that store received Concept Unification.
