This animatronic first premiered at the {{< wiki-link "Sacramento , CA (7020 Stockton Blvd)" >}} in 1980. He was the second cabaret act that {{< wiki-link "Pizza Time Theatre" >}} introduced, replacing {{< wiki-link "Dolli Dimples (Cabaret)" >}}.

Movements

Artie Antlers likely featured the same movements as the other cabaret characters, but removed Dolli Dimples' bosom mechanism.
