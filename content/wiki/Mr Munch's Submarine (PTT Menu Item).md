<b><i>Mr. Munch's Submarine</b></i> was a Pizza Time Theatre menu item introduced in 1979.<ref>https:<i>www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt</i>ptt_program_nc.pdf</ref><ref>https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/274053727/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22%20%2299%22%20%22hot%20dogs%22%20%221%20free%20token%22%20&match=1</ref>

The item was a submarine sandwich with three meats, two cheeses, and lettuce.<ref name=':0'>https://texasarchive.org/2017_00873</ref>

<h2> History </h2>
It is unknown the price of the item upon its release. In 1980 its price was somewhere in the range of $1.59 to $2.65 (likely the latter).<ref>https:<i>www.newspapers.com/article/fort-worth-star-telegram/46498440/</ref> In April of 1982 it would be $3.45.<ref>https:</i>www.newspapers.com/article/the-cincinnati-post/98317675/</ref> In October 1983 it would be $3.79 in Honolulu and $3.25 in New York.<ref>https:<i>www.newspapers.com/article/press-and-sun-bulletin-johnson-city-ptt/32794950/</ref><ref>https:</i>www.newspapers.com/newspage/274053727/?terms=%22Kiddie%20Dog%22%20%22pizza%20time%20theatre%22%20%2299%22%20%22hot%20dogs%22%20%221%20free%20token%22%20&match=1</ref>

<h2> Recipe / Ingredients </h2>

* Hoagie Roll
* Salami
* Ham
* Pepperoni
* Swiss Cheese
* American Cheese
* Lettuce<ref name=':0' />
* Small Potato Chips Bag
** At the [Beaumont, TX (5874 Eastex Fwy)]({{< ref "wiki/Beaumont, TX (5874 Eastex Fwy).md" >}}) location, this was the Texas brand <b><i>Morton's.<ref name=':0' /></b></i>

The item is served in a woven food basket on top of either deli paper or wax paper. A toppings bar was available which offered onions, piccalilli, horseradish, and mustard.
