History

On February 27, 1998, an article was published stating that ShowBiz Pizza Time had plans to open a Chuck E. Cheese’s in Japan. Tokyo was selected as the first location, and was expected to open in early 1999 after a franchisee was selected.(1) Due to there being no other articles talking about the Tokyo store after this, it is assumed a franchisee was never found, and the plan for Chuck E. Cheese’s in Japan was called off.
