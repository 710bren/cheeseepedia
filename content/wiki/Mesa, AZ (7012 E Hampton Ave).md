**_Mesa, AZ (7012 E Hampton Ave)**_ opened as a _Chuck E. Cheese's_ in December 2003 with a [Studio C Beta, 16M]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.md" >}}), and closed on December 2, 2018. It was the second and last store in Mesa to open. The location is dubbed 'East Mesa' by fans.

## History ##
East Mesa opened in December 2003 with the [CEC 2005 Remodel Program Prototype]({{< ref "wiki/CEC 2005 Remodel Program.md" >}}) and the [Studio C Beta, 16M]({{< ref "wiki/Studio C Beta.md" >}}). By December 2015, the [Skytubes]({{< ref "wiki/Skytubes.md" >}}) were removed. This location closed on December 2, 2018. The space is used by Spirit Halloween during the months of September and October. Interestingly, the golden rims on the ceiling remain in tact.
