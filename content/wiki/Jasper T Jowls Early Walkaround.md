The Jasper T. Jowls Early Walkaround was a walkaround costume that would appear at the [1371 Kooser Road store]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd).md" >}}) located in the Blossom Hill Shopping Center in San Jose California, used alongside the Prototype Chuck Walkaround & Munch Early Walkaround.

## History ##
The Jasper T. Jowls Early Walkaround would debut at the 1371 Kooser Road Store in San Jose, California, from December 1978 to 1981, where it would be replaced by the 1981 Jasper T. Jowls walkaround. The costume would be used in several promotional photographs.
