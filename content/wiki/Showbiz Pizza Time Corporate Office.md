The **_Showbiz Pizza Time Corporate Office**_ (_4441 W Airport Fwy Ste 215, Irving, TX 75062_) was the main office used for _Showbiz Pizza Time Inc.,_ later named to _CEC Entertainment Inc._, from 1983 to July 2015. 

The office is now divided into different businesses and is now 'Airport Plaza'. CEC moved from this location to the [Chuck E. Cheese's Corporate Office]({{< ref "wiki/Chuck E Cheese's Corporate Office.md" >}}) in July of 2015.
