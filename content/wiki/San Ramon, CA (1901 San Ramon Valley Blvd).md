**_San Ramon, CA (1901 San Ramon Valley Blvd)**_ was a _Pizza Time Theatre_ that opened in October 1980 with a [Cyberamics Portrait / Shelf Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Portrait  Shelf Stage.md" >}}), and closed in 1984.

## History ##
During early to mid 1980, PTT Inc. was creating concept art for San Ramon as it was set to be the base for all future newly built stores<ref>https://www.showbizpizza.com/info/documents/ptt/ptt_pizzatimes-1980.pdf</ref><ref name=':0'>https://www.newspapers.com/article/oakland-tribune-pizza-time-theatre-san-r/141015533/</ref>. The store opened in October 1980 with a [Cyberamics Portrait / Shelf Stage]({{< ref "wiki/Cyberamics Portrait  Shelf Stage.md" >}}). The store was 9,600 square feet large<ref name=':0' />. Not much documentation exists of San Ramon after it's opening. The store closed in 1984.
