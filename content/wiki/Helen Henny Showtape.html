+++ title = "Helen Henny Showtape" draft = false tags = ["Showtapes"] date = "2023-08-06" [Article] startDate = "1977-8-12" endDate = "" contributors = ["The 64th Gamer","Ptt1977"] +++ {{Showtape |image=Wincehster_chuck_instore_bw.jpg |date=August 12, 1977 |pttreel=y}}The Helen Henny Showtape is a currently lost Pizza Time Theatre showtape that debuted at the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)]({{< ref "wiki/San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd).html" >}}) in August of 1977.

History

The confirmation of this showtapes existence is from the installation of [[Winchester Helen Henny (Animatronic)|Winchester Helen Henny]] at the Winchester location on August 12, 1977.https://archive.org/details/atariincbusiness0000gold/page/256/mode/2up The showtape would run until [Madame Oink I]({{< ref "wiki/Madame Oink I.html" >}}) replaced it in February of 1978.
