Cheese's that opened on April 28th, 2014 with a Circles Of Light. This location is a 'Phase 5' store with Rockstar artwork.

History

This location opened on April 28th, 2014, as a relocation of the old Little Rock store located on [[Little Rock, AR (10901 N Rodney Parham Road 303)|10901 N Rodney Parham Road 303]]. It opened with a Circles Of Light stage with no animatronic, being the first in Arkansas to have no animatronics.

Stages

The store opened with a Circles Of Light with no animatronic, which is still there to this day.
