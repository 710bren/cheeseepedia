The Big Cheese Birthday Show Dialogue Recording Session was a 2-track tape containing a showtape concept demo recording produced on October 7, 1976.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C2cQFyz12hA

The tape features 'The Big Cheese' (whom later on became }}">Chuck E. Cheese), and Crusty the Cat performing dialogue.

Production

The audio would be produced at Wally Heider Studio (245 Hyde St., San Francisco, CA, 94102). The script for the session was written by Robert “Bob” Allan Black under the direction of Atari, Gene Landrum, and Nolan Bushnell. The producer for the show would be Ed Bogus Productions (680 Beach St. Suite 495, San Francisco, CA 94109).[1]
